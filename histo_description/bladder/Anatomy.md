# Anatomy

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Allantois: rudimentary structure lined by endoderm that is connected to urachus
* Urachus: formed during descent of abdominal wall, connects umbilicus to apex (dome) of bladder, torn apart as embryo elongates but remnants persist in anterior abdominal wall and may persist in bladder wall (see [patent urachus](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/bladderurachus.html))
