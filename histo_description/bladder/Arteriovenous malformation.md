# Arteriovenous malformation

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Admixture of malformed vessels such as capillaries, arteries and venules
* Abrupt changes in thickness of medial and elastic layers of vessels, abnormal vascular dilation
* Often advanced small vessel disease, hemorrhage, ulceration ([Hum Pathol 1986;17:94](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/3943855))
* Involves submucosa but not muscularis propria
* May be associated with pseudocarcinomatous epithelial hyperplasia of bladder ([Am J Surg Pathol 2008;32:92](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/18162775))
