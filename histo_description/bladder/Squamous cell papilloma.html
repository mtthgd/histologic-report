Microscopic (histologic) description
  • Multiple, free floating papillary fronds with thin, well developed fibrovascular cores composed of bland, benign appearing squamous cells (Case Rep Pathol 2013;2013:486312)
  • No dysplasia seen
  • Stromal invasion is not seen
  • Hyperplasia and mitotic activity restricted to basal / parabasal layers (Case Rep Pathol 2013;2013:486312)
  • Koilocytes are rare or absent (Cancer 2000;88:1679)
