# Squamous cell papilloma

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Multiple, free floating papillary fronds with thin, well developed fibrovascular cores composed of bland, benign appearing squamous cells ([Case Rep Pathol 2013;2013:486312](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/24159399))
* No dysplasia seen
* Stromal invasion is not seen
* Hyperplasia and mitotic activity restricted to basal / parabasal layers ([Case Rep Pathol 2013;2013:486312](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/24159399))
* Koilocytes are rare or absent ([Cancer 2000;88:1679](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/10738227))
