# Disc material

Microscopic (histologic) description

* **Annular fibrosus**: collagen (pink)
* **Nucleus pulposus**: pure cartilage (blue)
* **Herniated disk**: vascular ingrowth
* Also chondrocyte proliferation, structural alterations in form of tears and clefts, granular changes and mucous degeneration ([BMC Res Notes 2011;4:497](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/22087871))
