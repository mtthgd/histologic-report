# Erdheim-Chester disease

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Soft tissue infiltrate of bland appearing histiocytes characterized by abundant foamy (xanthomatous) cytoplasm with surrounding fibrosis ([Mod Pathol 2018;31:581](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/29192649))
* Touton giant cells are frequently present
* Associated lymphoplasmacytic infiltrate is sparse
* Erdheim-Chester disease may be a component of mixed histiocytosis with Langerhans cell histiocytosis or Rosai-Dorfman disease ([Blood 2014;124:1119](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/24894769), [Hematologica 2020;105:e5](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/31123032))
