# Mesenchymal chondrosarcoma

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Solid sheets of undifferentiated small blue cells with a hemangiopericytoma-like vascular pattern mixed with islands of mature appearing, well differentiated hyaline cartilage
* Usually mitotic activity or necrosis present ([Arch Pathol Lab Med 2018;142:1421](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/30407856), [Arch Pathol Lab Med 2012;136:61](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/22208489))
