Microscopic (histologic) description
  • Chronic granulomatous inflammation with or without necrosis
  • Langhans type multinucleated giant cells is a helpful diagnostic feature
  • Fibrous tissue with dense lymphoplasmacytic infiltrate
  • Synovium is hyperplastic
  • Ziehl-Neelsen stain may or may not highlight acid fast bacilli (Eur J Case Rep Intern Med 2018;5:000870, Ann Saudi Med 1998;18:368, Int J Surg Case Rep 2021;88:106534, Case Rep Rheumatol 2016;2016:5718423)
