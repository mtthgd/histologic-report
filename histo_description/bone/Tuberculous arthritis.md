# Tuberculous arthritis

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Chronic granulomatous inflammation with or without necrosis
* Langhans type multinucleated giant cells is a helpful diagnostic feature
* Fibrous tissue with dense lymphoplasmacytic infiltrate
* Synovium is hyperplastic
* Ziehl-Neelsen stain may or may not highlight acid fast bacilli ([Eur J Case Rep Intern Med 2018;5:000870](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/30756044), [Ann Saudi Med 1998;18:368](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/17344697), [Int J Surg Case Rep 2021;88:106534](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/34700127), [Case Rep Rheumatol 2016;2016:5718423](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/27366339))
