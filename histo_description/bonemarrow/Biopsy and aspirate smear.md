# Biopsy and aspirate smear

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Bone marrow biopsies are helpful to determine cellularity and presence of fibrosis
* Purple granular deposits that impair evaluation of touch preparations are due to cartilage in biopsy and are more common in children ([J Clin Pathol 2003;56:883](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/14600144))
