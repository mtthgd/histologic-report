# AML with mutated iNPM1i

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Wide range of morphologies are seen in AML with *NPM1* mutation ([N Engl J Med 2005;352:254](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/15659725))
* There is a strong association between both acute myelomonocytic and acute monocytic leukemia and *NPM1* mutation ([Blood 2007;109:874](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/17008539))
* *NPM1* mutations are also detected in AML with and without maturation and in pure erythroid leukemia
* Cup-like nuclei are highly associated with the presence of NPM1 and *FLT3* internal tandem duplication mutations ([Cancer 2009;115:5481](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/19672946))
