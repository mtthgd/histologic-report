# Nonsecretory myeloma

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Mature plasma cells with abundant basophilic cytoplasm, perinuclear hofs (prominent Golgi apparatus), eccentric nuclei and clock face chromatin without nucleoli
* Immature plasma cells with dispersed nuclear chromatin, high N:C ratio and a prominent nucleolus ([J Chin Med Assoc 2011;74:464](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/22036139))
* In trephine biopsy, plasma cells may form interstitial clusters, focal nodules or diffuse sheets
