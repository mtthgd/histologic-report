# Amyloid

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Amorphous eosinophilic extracellular deposits in fat, stroma or vessels
* May cause ductal atrophy, form rings around individual fat cells, have associated multinucleated giant cell reaction or osseous metaplasia ([J Clin Path 2002;55:634](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/12147664))
