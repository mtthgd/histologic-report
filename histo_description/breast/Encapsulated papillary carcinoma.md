# Encapsulated papillary carcinoma

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Papillary tumor with pushing borders, may be within a cystically dilated duct, surrounded by a thick fibrous capsule ([Histopathology 2008;52:20](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/18171414), [Mod Pathol 2021;34:1044](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/33462367), [Virchows Arch 2022;480:5](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/34292391))
* Delicate papillary fronds with fibrovascular cores lined by cuboidal to columnar epithelial cells with low to intermediate grade atypia
* Cribriform and solid architecture may be present
* Low mitotic activity (average 3 mitoses per 10 high power fields [HPFs]) ([Am J Surg Pathol 2011;35:1](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/21084964))
* Most show complete lack of myoepithelial cells along the papillae and around the periphery of the tumor ([Am J Surg Pathol 2006;30:1002](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/16861972), [Am J Surg Pathol 2011;35:1](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/21084964))
* Basement membrane markers (collagen IV, laminin) may be expressed around the periphery ([Pathobiology 2021;88:359](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/34265775), [Am J Clin Pathol 2009;131:228](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/19141383))
* Invasive carcinoma is defined as the presence of neoplastic cells infiltrating beyond fibrous capsule ([Mod Pathol 2021;34:1044](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/33462367))
  + Most commonly invasive carcinoma of no special type (IDC NST), small (pT1), ER+, HER2- and low grade ([Am J Surg Pathol 2011;35:1093](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/21753694), [Breast J 2021;27:209](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/33389809), [Breast J 2019;25:539](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/30972862), [Curr Probl Cancer 2018;42:291](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/29731165))
  + Staging is based on the size of the conventional invasive component if present
  + Epithelial displacement / entrapment of neoplastic cells within capsule can occur and must be distinguished from true invasion beyond the fibrous capsule
* High grade invasive carcinoma with EPC features ([Histopathology 2015;66:740](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/25382726), [Histol Histopathol 2019;34:137](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/30004109))
  + High grade cytologic atypia with nuclear pleomorphism, frequent mitotic figures (mean mitotic activity 22 per 10 HPFs) and occasional necrosis
  + More likely to be ER- or triple negative
