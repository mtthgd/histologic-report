Microscopic (histologic) description
  • Dilated glands lined by flat cuboidal or columnar cells
  • Epithelium layer may be attenuated or absent
  • Cyst wall may show dense fibrosis and scattered inflammatory cells
  • May rupture and elicit inflammatory response with foamy macrophages, cholesterol clefts and fibrosis (Breast Cancer Res Treat 2002;75:213, Breast Cancer Res Treat 2006;97:115)
