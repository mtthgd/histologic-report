Microscopic (histologic) description
  • Irregularly shaped and sized Müllerian type glands, surrounded by variable amounts of bland smooth muscle (Int J Gynecol Pathol 2014;33:330)
  • Lobular arrangement of large glands that may show papillary infolding; purely endocervical glands are usually smaller
  • Epithelium lining the glands may show tubal, endometrioid, squamous and (rarely) mesonephric differentiation (Int J Gynecol Pathol 2000;19:195)
  • Smooth muscle component merges with the preexisting wall of smooth muscle at the border of the lesion
  • Bland cytology and absent / rare mitoses
  • No desmoplastic response is evident
