# Adenomyoma

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Irregularly shaped and sized Müllerian type glands, surrounded by variable amounts of bland smooth muscle ([Int J Gynecol Pathol 2014;33:330](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/24901392))
* Lobular arrangement of large glands that may show papillary infolding; purely endocervical glands are usually smaller
* Epithelium lining the glands may show tubal, endometrioid, squamous and (rarely) mesonephric differentiation ([Int J Gynecol Pathol 2000;19:195](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/10907166))
* Smooth muscle component merges with the preexisting wall of smooth muscle at the border of the lesion
* Bland cytology and absent / rare mitoses
* No desmoplastic response is evident
