# Diffuse low grade glioma MAPK pathway altered

Microscopic (histologic) description

* General features: infiltrative, glial tumor cells without a neuronal component
* *BRAF* V600 altered tumors tend to have densely fibrillary areas and microcalcifications ([Acta Neuropathol 2015;130:575](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/26264609))
* *FGFR1* altered tumors tend to have oligodendrocyte-like cells, rare or no mitoses, no necrosis and no microvascular proliferation ([Acta Neuropathol 2016;131:833](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/26810070))
* In a large study excluding NF1 patients, 5.9% of pediatric low grade gliomas exhibited astrocytic differentiation and 3.0% exhibited oligodendroglial differentiation ([Cancer Cell 2020;37:569](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/32289278))
