# Apoptotic colopathy

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Reduced intracellular mucin, epithelial apoptosis and sloughing, increase in mitotic figures ([Hum Pathol 1998;29:972](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/9744314), [Cesk Patol 2011;47:130](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/21887933))
* May have erosion of superficial epithelium, consistent with clinical impression of aphthoid ulcer
* Also increased lymphocytes and neutrophils ([Dis Colon Rectum 2006;49:109](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/16273330))
* Patchy mononuclear infiltration in upper part of lamina propria, increased epithelial cell proliferation of individual crypts, basal cryptitis ([Cesk Patol 2010;46:37](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/21275224))
* Rarely lamina propria edema or extravasated red blood cells
