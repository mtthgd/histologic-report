# Diarrhea  dysentery

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Patchy lesions with variable villus abnormality, rarely severe
* Increased chronic and acute inflammatory infiltrate in epithelium and lamina propria
* Small intestinal bacterial overgrowth: common cause of chronic diarrhea and malabsorption with either villous blunting or normal duodenal biopsy ([Arch Pathol Lab Med 2010;134:264](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/20121616))
