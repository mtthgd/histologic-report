# Focal active colitis

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Neutrophil mediated surface epithelial injury, neutrophils within epithelial cells (cryptitis) or within crypt lumina (crypt abscess) ([Biomedicines 2023;11:2631](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/37893005))
* Expansion of lamina propria with neutrophils and eosinophils
* Well preserved crypt architecture
