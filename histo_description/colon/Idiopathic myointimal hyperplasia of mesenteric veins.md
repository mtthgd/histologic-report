# Idiopathic myointimal hyperplasia of mesenteric veins

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Biopsy findings: ischemic mucosal change accompanied by proliferation of hyperplastic, thick walled, hyalinized blood vessels ([Am J Surg Pathol 1996;20:1271](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/8827035))
* Resection findings: mesenteric and mural veins show myointimal hyperplasia, making them somewhat resemble arteries; no vasculitis present; arteries are unaffected ([Gastroenterology 1991;101:533](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/2065929))
