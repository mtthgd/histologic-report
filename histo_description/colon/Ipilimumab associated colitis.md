# Ipilimumab associated colitis

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Lymphoplasmacytic expansion of lamina propria; increased apoptosis and intraepithelial lymphocytes; cryptitis and crypt elongation ([Am J Surg Pathol 2008;32:1130](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/18545145))
* Severe cases may show ulceration/perforation, pseudopolyp formation and crypt dilation, without lymphocytosis or apoptosis ([J Clin Gastroenterol 2013;47:781](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/23632354))
