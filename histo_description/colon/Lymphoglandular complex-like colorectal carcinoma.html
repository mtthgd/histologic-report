Microscopic (histologic) description
  • Irregular intestinal type glands confined to lymphoid stroma with germinal centers are frequently seen
  • Glands often show angulation, fusion or nesting
  • Single cell infiltration and desmoplasia are not always present
  • Tumors are typically low grade and goblet cells are infrequent in invasive component
  • No lamina propria in invasive component
  • Lymphovascular invasion and perineural invasion rare
  • Surface precursor 90% of the time, either adenoma with or without high grade dysplasia or sessile serrated lesion with dysplasia
  • While the majority are confined to submucosa, a subset can extend into muscularis propria or beyond into pericolorectal tissue
  • Lymph node metastasis in a subset of cases
  • Reference: Am J Surg Pathol 2024;48:70
