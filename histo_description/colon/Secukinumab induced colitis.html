Microscopic (histologic) description
  • Active colitis with or without ulceration
  • Prominent apoptotic bodies
  • Crypt withering, fibrinoid necrosis of small vessels and ischemic pattern of injury occasionally reported (Inflamm Bowel Dis 2022;28:e47)
  • Lymphocytic colitis pattern has also been reported (J Investig Med High Impact Case Rep 2022;10:23247096221110399)
  • Collagenous colitis pattern (Cureus 2021;13:e16147)
  • Well developed features of chronic injury such as crypt architectural distortion and paneth cell metaplasia are often absent
