# Secukinumab induced colitis

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Active colitis with or without ulceration
* Prominent apoptotic bodies
* Crypt withering, fibrinoid necrosis of small vessels and ischemic pattern of injury occasionally reported ([Inflamm Bowel Dis 2022;28:e47](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/34792606))
* Lymphocytic colitis pattern has also been reported ([J Investig Med High Impact Case Rep 2022;10:23247096221110399](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/35801542))
* Collagenous colitis pattern ([Cureus 2021;13:e16147](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/34367765))
* Well developed features of chronic injury such as crypt architectural distortion and paneth cell metaplasia are often absent
