# Eosinophilic esophagitis

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Major criterion: ≥ 15 eosinophils per high power field (40x magnification) present in the squamous mucosa ([Arch Pediatr 2019;26:182](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/30827775))
* Minor criteria: eosinophils concentrated in the surface epithelium as opposed to the base, extreme basal zone hyperplasia, eosinophilic microabscesses, eosinophil degranulation, surface desquamation, lamina propria fibrosis ([Arch Pediatr 2019;26:182](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/30827775))
