# Calcineurin Inhibitor toxicity

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Toxicity (in 1%) of tacrolimus is similar to cyclosporin A at level of renal vascular endothelium, leading to fibrin thrombi in glomerular capillaries and afferent arterioles ([Am J Surg Pathol 1996;20:306](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/8772784), [Am J Surg Pathol 1993;17:60](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/7680544))
* May also cause arteriolar hyalinosis and splitting / reduplication of glomerular basement membrane
