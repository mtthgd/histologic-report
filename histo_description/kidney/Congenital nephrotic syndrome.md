# Congenital nephrotic syndrome

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Cystically dilated proximal and distal tubes with an attenuated tubular epithelium, microcytic changes ([Int J Pediatr Nephrol 1980;1:10](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/7343522))
* May be minimal glomerular changes in early disease
* With disease progression may see mesangial hypercellularity, increased mesangial matrix
* Rare immature glomeruli often present
* Focal segmental glomerulosclerosis (FSGS) or global glomerulosclerosis ([Int J Pediatr Nephrol 1980;1:10](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/7343522))
* Interstitial fibrosis and tubular atrophy with progressive disease
