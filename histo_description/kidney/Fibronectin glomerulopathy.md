# Fibronectin glomerulopathy

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Lobular accentuation of glomeruli with minimal hypercellularity ([Nihon Jinzo Gakkai Shi 1999;41:49](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/10361419))
* Marked enlargement of mesangium and subendothelial space due to massive deposition of fibronectin and fibulin (PAS+, Congo red negative homogenous substance, [Mod Pathol 2012;25:709](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/22282304)), causing obliteration of capillary lumens
