# Membranous nephropathy

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Glomeruli may appear entirely normal in early disease (stage 1)
* Thickening of glomerular basement membrane
* Subepithelial spike formation or vacuolated appearance on PAS or Jones silver stain ([Colvin: Diagnostic Pathology - Kidney Diseases, 2nd Edition, 2015](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0323377076/pathologyoutl-20), [Zhou: Silva's Diagnostic Renal Pathology, 2nd Edition, 2017](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/1316613984/pathologyoutl-20))
