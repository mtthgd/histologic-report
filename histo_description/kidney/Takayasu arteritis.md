# Takayasu arteritis

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Granulomatous vasculitis with mixed inflammatory infiltrate, including giant cells, with
  necrosis, disruption of the elastic lamina and features of recanalization ([Int J Nephrol Renovasc Dis 2018;11:225](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/30147353))
* Varying renal vascular and parenchymal changes associated with the degree of chronic hypertension and renal ischemia: atherosclerosis, ischemic change, atrophy, infiltrating lymphocytes, tubular dilation and narrowing and glomerulosclerosis
* Specific glomerular and tubulointerstitial changes are rare and often manifestations of chronic disease ([Chin Med Sci J 2009;24:69](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/19618601))
* Reported glomerular disease includes: **mesangial proliferative glomerulonephritis**, [IgA nephropathy](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/kidneyiga.html), [membranous glomerulonephropathy](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/kidneymemgn.html), [membranoproliferative glomerulonephropathy](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/kidneymembranoprolif.html), [crescentic glomerulonephropathy](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/kidneyrpgn.html), [renal amyloidosis](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/kidneyamyloidosis.html) and [focal glomerulosclerosis](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/kidneyfsgs.html) ([Clin Exp Rheumatol 2007;25:S10](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/17428356))
