# Budd-Chiari syndrome

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Severe centrilobular congestion / necrosis, progressing to centrilobular fibrosis
* Sinusoidal dilation, portal tract expansion / fibrosis and ductular reaction in zones 1 and 3 may be seen ([Am J Surg Pathol 2014;38:205](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/24418854))
* Large regenerative nodules, focal nodular hyperplasia and hepatocellular adenomas can develop ([Histopathology 2004;44:172](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/14764061))
