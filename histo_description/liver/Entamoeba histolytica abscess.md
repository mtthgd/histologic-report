# Entamoeba histolytica abscess

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Abundant necrotic debris with amebic organisms and mononuclear inflammation most frequently found at the edge of the lesion
* Trophozoites (40 - 60 micrometers) have round, eccentric nuclei and abundant eosinophilic to amphophilic foamy cytoplasm, which may harbor ingested red blood cells
