# Fasciola hepatica

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Dilated intrahepatic bile ducts may contain adult flukes
* Tract shaped necrotic debris
* Eosinophils
* Charcot-Leyden crystals ([Turk Patoloji Derg 2019;35:58](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/28272650)​)
* Eosinophilic granulomas: center of dense inflammatory cells composed predominantly of eosinophils with scattered Charcot-Leyden crystals surrounded by a palisade of granulomatous (histiocytic) response ([Am J Surg Pathol 2001;25:1316](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/11688468))
* Histiocytes
* Rarely, eggs ([Burt: MacSween's Pathology of the Liver, 7th Edition, 2017](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/%200702066974/pathologyoutl-20))
