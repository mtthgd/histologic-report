# Lysosomal acid lipase deficiency in liver biopsies

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Diffuse microvesicular steatosis in the hepatocytes
* Foamy macrophages, containing lipid and ceroid, are present in the sinusoids and portal tracts
* Large Kupffer cells with increased vacuoles, lipid droplets or cholesterol crystals
* Progresses to fibrosis and to micronodular cirrhosis ([Histopathology 2012;60:1107](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/22621222))
