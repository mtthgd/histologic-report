# Portal hypertension

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Findings vary depending on causative disease; changes may be those of [cirrhosis](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/livercirrhosis.html), [schistosomiasis](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/liverschisto.html),
  [nodular regenerative hyperplasia](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/livertumornodularregenhyper.html), [hepatoportal sclerosis](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/liverhepatoportalsclerosis.html), [granulomatous disease](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/livergrannoninfect.html), outflow obstruction or others
* Collapsed portal vein radicles may be seen in pediatric patients ([Pediatr Dev Pathol 2003;6:421](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/14708735))
