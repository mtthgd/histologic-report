# Alveolar proteinosis

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Alveoli are expanded by amorphous eosinophilic and coarsely granular material consisting of surfactant material and cellular debris from type II pneumocytes and necrotic alveolar macrophages
* Cholesterol clefts and foamy macrophages may be present and associated with the exudate
* Associated type 2 pneumocyte hyperplasia may be present
* Absent to mild associated interstitial fibrosis with minimal associated chronic inflammation ([Lancet Respir Med 2018;6:554](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/29397349))
