# Fetal

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Irregular tubular structures of columnar epithelial cells with clear cytoplasm and oval nuclei, optically clear nuclei rich in biotin
* Resembles fetal lung in pseudoglandular stage
* High grade: disorganized glands, large vesicular nuclei, prominent nucleoli, pronounced anisonucleosis, transition to conventional adenocarcinoma, broad areas of necrosis, desmoplastic stroma, p53+, AFP+, no morules ([Am J Surg Pathol 1998;22:399](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/9537466))
