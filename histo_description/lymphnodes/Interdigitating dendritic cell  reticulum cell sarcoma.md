# Interdigitating dendritic cell  reticulum cell sarcoma

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Pleomorphic spindled or epithelioid cells arranged in fascicles, whorls or sheet-like patterns, often with indistinct cell borders and are characterized by large nuclei, vesicular chromatin, distinct nucleoli and abundant eosinophilic cytoplasm ([Am J Clin Pathol 2001;115:589](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/11293908))
* Paracortical distribution or diffuse effacement in lymph nodes
* Occasional multinucleated forms may be present
* Variable degree of admixed inflammatory cells, primarily lymphocytes and plasma cells
