Microscopic (histologic) description
  • Lesion generally not evident on routine H&E stained sections (Arch Pathol Lab Med 2018;142:1330)
  • Lymph node architecture is intact
  • Involved follicles are generally normal in size
  • Neoplastic cells (BCL2 strong+, CD10 strong+) are exclusively centrocytes, confined to the follicle centers (Cancers (Basel) 2023;15:785)
  • Individual germinal centers show varying degrees of involvement
  • By definition, no interfollicular infiltration is seen (Blood 2011;118:2976)
  • Involved follicles show low proliferation fraction with Ki67
