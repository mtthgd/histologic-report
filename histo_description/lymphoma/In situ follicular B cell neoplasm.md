# In situ follicular B cell neoplasm

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Lesion generally not evident on routine H&E stained sections ([Arch Pathol Lab Med 2018;142:1330](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/30221980))
* Lymph node architecture is intact
* Involved follicles are generally normal in size
* Neoplastic cells (BCL2 strong+, CD10 strong+) are exclusively centrocytes, confined to the follicle centers ([Cancers (Basel) 2023;15:785](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/36765742))
* Individual germinal centers show varying degrees of involvement
* By definition, no interfollicular infiltration is seen ([Blood 2011;118:2976](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/21768298))
* Involved follicles show low proliferation fraction with Ki67
