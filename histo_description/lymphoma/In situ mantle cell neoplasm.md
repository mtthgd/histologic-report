# In situ mantle cell neoplasm

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Lymphoid architecture preserved with no or slight mantle zone expansion ([Blood 2016;127:2375](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/26980727), [Semin Diagn Pathol 2018;35:76](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/29129357))
* Cyclin D1 positive B cells identified within the mantle zone ([Blood 2016;127:2375](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/26980727), [Virchows Arch 2016;468:245](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/26298543), [Haematologica 2012;97:270](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/22058203))
  + Usually restricted to the inner mantle zone
  + Rarely scattered throughout the mantle zone or present in the outer mantle zone
  + Very rarely intrafollicular or focally within germinal centers
* May involve multiple follicles in a lymph node ([Am J Surg Pathol 2016;40:943](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/26945339))
* May be identified in association with another lymphoma ([Blood 2016;127:2375](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/26980727))
