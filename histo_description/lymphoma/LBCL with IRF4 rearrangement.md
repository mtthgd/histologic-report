# LBCL with IRF4 rearrangement

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Lymph node architecture is effaced with a diffuse, diffuse and follicular or follicular infiltrate ([Blood 2011;118:139](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/21487109))
* Neoplastic follicles are large and arranged back to back with attenuated mantle zones ([Blood 2011;118:139](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/21487109))
* Neoplastic cells are medium to large ([Blood 2011;118:139](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/21487109))
* Open chromatin with small nucleoli ([Blood 2011;118:139](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/21487109))
* Absent starry sky pattern and infrequent mitotic figures ([Blood 2011;118:139](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/21487109))
