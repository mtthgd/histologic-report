# Nodular lymphocyte predominant B cell lymphoma  nodular lymphocyte predominant Hodgkin lymphoma

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Usually total replacement of nodal architecture by expansive vague nodules of small lymphocytes with sparse, relatively large tumor cells exhibiting multilobulated or round nuclei, thin nuclear membrane, finely granular chromatin and variable small nucleoli (popcorn cells) ([Semin Hematol 2016;53:190](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/27496311), [Clin Lymphoma Myeloma 2009;9:206](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/19525189))
* May have a rim of normal lymph node
* Large cells are called LP cells; previously known as lymphocytic and histiocytic cells
* LP cells are admixed with numerous small B lymphocytes, epithelioid histiocytes and CD21+ follicular dendritic cells
* Often PD-1+, T cell rosettes surrounding LP cells ([Am J Surg Pathol 2008;32:1252](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/18594468), [Blood 2017;129:802](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/28183747))
* Scant eosinophils, plasma cells or fibrosis
* Prominent sclerosis is unusual but may occur in older lesions
* Small germinal centers are rare between the nodules and usually present only at the compressed edge of normal lymph node tissue
* No / rare classic Reed-Sternberg cells, although LP cells may occasionally resemble Reed-Sternberg cells / variants
* No well formed granulomas, although epithelioid histiocytes may be prominent
* Diffuse patterns with background of reactive T cells
* 6 immunoarchitectural patterns have been described ([Am J Surg Pathol 2003;27:1346](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/14508396))
  + B cell rich nodular (typical / classic)
  + Serpiginous / interconnected B cell rich
  + Extranodular LP cells (variant)
  + T cell rich nodular
  + Diffuse T cell rich
  + Diffuse B cell rich
* Variant patterns are associated with disease recurrence and transformation ([Br J Haematol 2018;181:403](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/28369705), [Br J Haematol 2014;167:238](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/24965443))
* Nodular lymphocyte predominant Hodgkin lymphoma and T cell histiocyte rich large B cell lymphoma are considered to be parts of the same disease spectrum ([PLoS One 2013;8:e78812](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/24244368), [Br J Haematol 2015;169:415](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/25644177))
* Syncytial variant is rarely reported ([Am J Surg Pathol 2009;33:1725](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/19730363))
* Rare cases with features overlapping between T cell rich NLPHL and classic Hodgkin lymphoma (CHL) (i.e., neoplastic cells expressing both B cell program and CHL markers) have been reported ([Arch Pathol Lab Med 2023 Dec 7 [Epub ahead of print]](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/38059511))
* Bone marrow involvement: 3% of cases; involvement by large B cells (< 10% of all cases) in background of T cells and histiocytes
