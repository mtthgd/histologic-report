# Primary effusion lymphoma

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Large pleomorphic cells with irregular nuclear contours, condensed chromatin, prominent nucleoli and abundant cytoplasm ([Diagn Cytopathol 2006;34:335](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/16604559), [Cancer 2007;111:224](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/17554754))
* Some of the cells may have cytoplasmic vacuoles or multinucleation
* In nodal and extranodal location, effacement of the architecture
