Microscopic (histologic) description
  • Lined by pseudostratified ciliated columnar (respiratory type) epithelium
  • Areas of squamous metaplasia, atrophic squamous (squamoid) or simple cuboidal cells may be seen (Int J Oral Maxillofac Surg 1987;16:682)
  • Mucous (goblet) cells are not uncommon
  • Cyst wall is composed of loose fibrous connective tissue, which may be inflamed
  • Subepithelial fibrosis or hyalinization of cyst wall is common (Int J Oral Maxillofac Surg 1988;17:282, Oral Surg Oral Med Oral Pathol 1986;62:544)
  • Reactive bone formation may be present
