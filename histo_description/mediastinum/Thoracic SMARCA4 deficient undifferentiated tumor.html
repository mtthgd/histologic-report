Microscopic (histologic) description
  • Sheets of variably discohesive, large round to epithelioid cells with vesicular chromatin and prominent nucleoli
    • Nuclei are relatively monotonous, with occasional cells displaying mild to moderate pleomorphism (Mod Pathol 2017;30:797, Am J Surg Pathol 2019;43:455, J Thorac Oncol 2020;15:231)
  • Undifferentiated or poorly differentiated morphology with occasional rhabdoid features
    • Rhabdoid cells may be present, although they are often a focal finding and may be absent in small specimens (Mod Pathol 2017;30:797, Am J Surg Pathol 2019;43:455, J Thorac Oncol 2020;15:231)
  • Markedly increased mitotic activity (Mod Pathol 2017;30:797, Am J Surg Pathol 2019;43:455, J Thorac Oncol 2020;15:231)
  • Extensive necrosis (Mod Pathol 2017;30:797, Am J Surg Pathol 2019;43:455, J Thorac Oncol 2020;15:231)
  • Rare findings include spindling, myxoid change, sclerosis, alveolar pattern and clear cell change (Mod Pathol 2017;30:797, Am J Surg Pathol 2019;43:455, J Thorac Oncol 2020;15:231)
  • Unequivocal evidence of epithelial differentiation (e.g., gland, papilla or keratinization) is absent in most cases but ~5% of cases show a combined histology with juxtaposed conventional NSCLC (J Thorac Oncol 2020;15:231)
