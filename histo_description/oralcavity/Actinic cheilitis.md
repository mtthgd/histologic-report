# Actinic cheilitis

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Surface epithelium is variably dysplastic
* Hyperkeratosis, acanthosis and epithelial atrophy may be noted
* Underlying connective tissue exhibits solar elastosis, which appears as a band of amorphous, acellular, basophilic change due to ultraviolet light induced degeneration of collagen and elastin fibers ([Head Neck Pathol 2021;15:566](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/33372240))
* Chronic inflammatory cell infiltrate and dilated blood vessels are often present
