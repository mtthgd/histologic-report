# Adenomatoid hyperplasia of minor glands

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Multiple aggregates, varying in size, of normal appearing mucous acini surrounded by fibrous connective tissue stroma
* Normal ductal structures
* Overlying palatal epithelium is usually normal but may have lichenoid reaction or pseudoepitheliomatous hyperplasia
* Occasionally, areas of interstitial mucous extravasation with associated inflammatory reaction ([Barnes: Surgical Pathology of the Head and Neck, 3rd Edition, 2008 [p500]](http://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0849390230/pathologyoutl-20)) but typically no significant inflammatory cell infiltrates ([Oral Surg Oral Med Oral Pathol 1991;71:583](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/2047099))
