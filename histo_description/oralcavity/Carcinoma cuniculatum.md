# Carcinoma cuniculatum

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Architecturally complex proliferation of well differentiated squamous epithelium that branches and forms keratin filled cysts or cavities
* Cavities resemble a rabbit burrow
* There is a predominantly endophytic growth pattern ([Head Neck Pathol 2022;16:213](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/34076846))
* Minimal or no cytologic atypia is observed
* Rete pegs are broad and bulbous ([Head Neck Pathol 2022;16:213](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/34076846))
