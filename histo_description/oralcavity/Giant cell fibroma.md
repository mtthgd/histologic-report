# Giant cell fibroma

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Varying numbers of large, plump angular and stellate fibroblasts that may demonstrate multiple nuclei in the superficial connective tissue ([Oral Surg Oral Med Oral Pathol 1974;37:374](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/4521457))
* Proliferation of collagen bundles
* Overlying surface epithelium with narrow and elongated epithelial rete ridges
* Absence of inflammation
