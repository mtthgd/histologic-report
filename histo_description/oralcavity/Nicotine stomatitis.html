Microscopic (histologic) description
  • Keratosis and acanthosis of surface epithelium (Br J Cancer 1971;25:403)
  • Mild, patchy, chronic inflammation of the connective tissue stroma and minor seromucous glands
    • Inflammatory excretion may be seen within the lumina of striated salivary ducts (Br J Cancer 1971;25:403)
  • Squamous metaplasia of ductal epithelium near the orifice may be seen in instances of papular elevation (Oral Surg Oral Med Oral Pathol 1965;20:306)
