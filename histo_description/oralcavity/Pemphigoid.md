# Pemphigoid

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Surface epithelium cleanly detached from the underlying connective tissue at the point of the basement membrane zone
* Superficial lamina propria contains a variable amount of inflammation that may contain lymphocytes, eosinophils and neutrophils ([Br J Oral Maxillofac Surg 2008;46:358](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/17804127))
* Aggregates of erythrocytes may be present in area of bullae formation
