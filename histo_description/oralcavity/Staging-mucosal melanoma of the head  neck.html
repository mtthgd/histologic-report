Microscopic (histologic) description
  • AJCC staging for mucosal melanomas does not provide for the histologic definition of a T3 lesion; as the majority of mucosal melanomas are invasive at presentation, mucosal based melanomas (T3 lesions) include those lesions that involve either the epithelium or lamina propria of the involved site
  • Rare examples of in situ mucosal melanomas occur but in situ mucosal melanomas are excluded from staging, as they are extremely rare
  • Reference: CAP: Protocol for the Examination of Specimens from Patients with Cancers of the Oral Cavity [Accessed 26 July 2024]
