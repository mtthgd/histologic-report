# Cystic endocrine tumors

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Typically appears as a thick fibrous wall with neuroendocrine cells lining the central cystic space and sometimes forming nests or vesicular structures within the fibrosis ([Am J Surg Pathol 2001;25:752](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/11395552))
* Necrosis and lymphovascular invasion are uncommon
