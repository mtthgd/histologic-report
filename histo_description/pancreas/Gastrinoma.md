# Gastrinoma

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Nests of monotonous low grade neuroendocrine cells with salt and pepper nuclei and ample amphophilic cytoplasm, as with any other well differentiated neuroendocrine tumor
* Associated with pancreatic polypeptide cell hyperplasia ([Hum Pathol 1997;28:149](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/9023394))
* Nonneoplastic pancreas may show large islets and [nesidioblastosis](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/pancreasnesidio.html)
