# Molecular genetics of pancreatic ductal carcinoma

Microscopic (histologic) description

* MSI tumors more often show a medullary or colloid morphology ([Gut 2020 Apr 29 [Epub ahead of print]](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/32350089))
* Germline mutated *ATM* tumors more often show a mucinous / colloid histology ([Mod Pathol 2019;32:1806](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/31285527))
* Tumors with squamous features show a very poor prognosis ([Nat Med 2011;17:500](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/21460848), [Nature 2016;531:47](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/26909576))
