# Mucocele  ranula

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Pseudocyst with a fibrous tissue wall ([Am J Otolaryngol 2015;36:114](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/25459318))
* Mucous cyst (mucocele)
  + Extravasation mucocele
    - Pool of mucin surrounded by granulation tissue due to traumatically injured minor salivary glands
    - More common in young individuals and lower the lip
  + Ranula: blue domed cyst in sublingual area
  + Plunging ranula: a mucocele extending into the neck and above the hyoid bone
  + Retention mucocele
    - Mucous filled cyst lined by epithelial cells such as cuboidal
    - Older patients, the floor of mouth, inside of the cheek
* Reference: [Rosai: Rosai and Ackerman's Surgical Pathology, 9th Edition, 2004](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/9996000427/pathologyoutl-20)
