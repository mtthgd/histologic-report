Microscopic (histologic) description
  • Early stage lesions: neutrophilic folliculitis and perifolliculitis involving adnexal structures (Am J Dermatopathol 1990;12:109)
    • Can destroy sebaceous glands and adnexal structures
  • Late stage lesions: granulomatous folliculitis and perifolliculitis, often also presenting with plasmacytic infiltrate (Am J Dermatopathol 1990;12:109, Arch Dermatol 2000;136:479)
    • Areas of coalescing hypertrophic fibrosis
    • Presence of naked hair shafts (J Dermatol 2020;47:41)
    • Presence of multinucleated foreign body giant cells
    • Presence of hemosiderin
