Microscopic (histologic) description
  • Basophilic, finely granular or amorphous crystalline deposits within the skin or subcutaneous tissue (StatPearls: Calcinosis Cutis [Accessed 16 December 2024])
  • May have adjacent foreign body reaction defined by histiocytes and multinucleated giant cells
  • Calciphylaxis is diagnosed histologically by identifying calcifications in capillary sized vessels (N Engl J Med 2018;378:1704)
