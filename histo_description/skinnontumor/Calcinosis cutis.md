# Calcinosis cutis

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Basophilic, finely granular or amorphous crystalline deposits within the skin or subcutaneous tissue ([StatPearls: Calcinosis Cutis [Accessed 16 December 2024]](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/28846311))
* May have adjacent foreign body reaction defined by histiocytes and multinucleated giant cells
* Calciphylaxis is diagnosed histologically by identifying calcifications in capillary sized vessels ([N Engl J Med 2018;378:1704](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/29719190))
