# Molluscum contagiosum

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Cup shaped lesion with inverted lobules of hyperplastic squamous epithelium which expand into the underlying dermis ([StatPearls: Molluscum Contagiosum [Accessed 29 June 2020]](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/28722927))
* Henderson-Paterson or molluscum bodies: large (up to 35 microns) intracytoplasmic eosinophilic inclusion bodies ([Clin Cosmet Investig Dermatol 2019;12:373](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/31239742))
* At the granular layer, the bodies become increasingly hematoxyphilic and occupy the entire cell
* Chronic inflammatory infiltrate seen in regressing lesions
