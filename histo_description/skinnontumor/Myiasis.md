# Myiasis

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Histologically, a mixed acute and chronic inflammatory infiltrate composed of varying proportions of neutrophils (early), lymphocytes, plasma cells and histiocytes (late), admixed with eosinophils ([J Am Acad Dermatol 2004;50:S26](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/14726861))
* Histologically, larvae are characterized by an undulating chitinous exoskeleton and pigmented spines (setae) protruding from exoskeleton
