# WNT activated deep penetrating  plexiform melanocytoma nevus

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Melanocytic neoplasm that exhibits a wedge shaped or plexiform growth pattern with melanin pigmentation ([Am J Surg Pathol 1989;13:39](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/2909196), [J Cutan Pathol 1992;19:172](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/1401342), [Arch Dermatol 1993;129:328](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/8447669))
* Usually well circumscribed and symmetrical ([Biology (Basel) 2022;11:460](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/35336833))
* Extends into deep dermis or subcutis with no clear maturation with depth ([Am J Surg Pathol 1989;13:39](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/2909196), [J Cutan Pathol 1992;19:172](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/1401342), [Arch Dermatol 1993;129:328](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/8447669), [Arch Pathol Lab Med 2011;135:321](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/21366455))
* Can have encircling of blood vessels, adnexal structures, nerves ([Am J Surg Pathol 1989;13:39](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/2909196), [J Cutan Pathol 1992;19:172](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/1401342), [Arch Dermatol 1993;129:328](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/8447669))
* Cellular, nested or fascicular with abundant pigment ([Am J Surg Pathol 1989;13:39](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/2909196), [Histopathology 2003;43:529](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/14636253))
* Lesions may be entirely intradermal, have an inconspicuous junctional component or uncommonly exhibit prominent junctional nests ([Am J Surg Pathol 1989;13:39](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/2909196), [Arch Pathol Lab Med 2011;135:321](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/21366455))
* Grenz zone may be present ([Biology (Basel) 2022;11:460](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/35336833), [Arch Pathol Lab Med 2011;135:321](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/21366455), [J Am Acad Dermatol 2014;71:1234](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/25175710))
* May be combined with other nevi
* Melanocytes are epithelioid to spindled
* Nuclei are typically round to fusiform
* Cytoplasm may exhibit granular pigment and nuclear pseudoinclusions
* C shaped melanocytes surrounding epithelioid nests can be seen
* Occasional dermal mitotic figures (nonatypical, < 1 - 2/mm2) ([J Cutan Pathol 1992;19:172](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/1401342), [Arch Pathol Lab Med 2011;135:321](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/21366455), [J Am Acad Dermatol 2014;71:1234](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/25175710))
* Occasional mild cytologic atypia can be seen including mild pleomorphism and hyperchromasia, variation in size, small nucleoli ([Am J Surg Pathol 1989;13:39](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/2909196), [Biology (Basel) 2022;11:460](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/35336833), [J Cutan Pathol 1992;19:172](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/1401342), [J Am Acad Dermatol 2014;71:1234](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/25175710))
* Atypical features in DPN include increased cytologic and architectural atypia
  + 1 - 3 mitotic figures/mm2, increased nuclear pleomorphism, increased N:C ratio; cherry red nucleoli ([Biology (Basel) 2022;11:460](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/35336833), [Eur J Dermatol 2014;24:594](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/25118781), [Cancers (Basel) 2021;13:3066](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/34205480), [J Cutan Pathol 2020;47:1150](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/32526042))
  + Asymmetrical, hypercellular, infiltrative borders ([Biology (Basel) 2022;11:460](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/35336833), [Eur J Dermatol 2014;24:594](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/25118781), [Cancers (Basel) 2021;13:3066](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/34205480), [J Cutan Pathol 2020;47:1150](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/32526042))
  + These lesions have been associated with lymph node metastasis ([Eur J Dermatol 2014;24:594](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/25118781), [Cancers (Basel) 2021;13:3066](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/34205480), [J Cutan Pathol 2020;47:1150](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/32526042), [Biology (Basel) 2022;11:460](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/35336833))
  + Can have increased Ki67 proliferation index ([Biology (Basel) 2022;11:460](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/35336833))
