# Cylindroma

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Compact nests of basaloid cells that fit together like a jigsaw puzzle, surrounded by thick basement membrane ([Dermatol Clin 2017;35:61](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/27890238))
* Cylindrical shape appreciated in cross section ([Dermatol Clin 2017;35:61](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/27890238))
* 2 groups of cells:
  + Undifferentiated peripheral palisading epithelial cells with small dark nuclei
  + Centrally located increasingly differentiated ductal cells with large pale nuclei ([Dermatol Clin 2017;35:61](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/27890238))
