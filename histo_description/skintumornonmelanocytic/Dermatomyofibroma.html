Microscopic (histologic) description
  • Somewhat ill defined proliferation of moderately cellular, bland spindled cells arranged in fascicles parallel to the epidermis (J Cutan Pathol 1992;19:85, J Cutan Pathol 2011;38:967)
  • No cytologic atypia or mitoses should be present
  • Adnexal structures appear trapped but spared (J Cutan Pathol 2011;38:967)
  • Bulk of neoplasm is present within the dermis
  • Tumor may brush the subcutis but overt involvement is unusual (Am J Dermatopathol 2009;31:44)
