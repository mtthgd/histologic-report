# Desmoplastic fibroblastoma

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Well circumscribed but may infiltrate fat or less commonly skeletal muscles ([Hum Pathol 1998;29:676](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/9670823))
* Hypocellular with fibromyxoid or collagenous stroma
* Bland spindled to stellate shaped fibroblasts
* Minimal vasculature
* Minimal to absent mitotic activity
