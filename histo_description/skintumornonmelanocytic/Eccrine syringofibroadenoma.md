# Eccrine syringofibroadenoma

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Proliferation of thin anastomosing reticulated epithelial cords and strands often forming a lattice and extending from the epidermis into the dermis ([J Cutan Aesthet Surg 2022;15:335](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/36561398))
* Lesional cells are monomorphous, basaloid, cuboidal and slightly smaller than adjacent keratinocytes
* Epithelial cords contain structures resembling eccrine ducts ([Ann Dermatol 2020;32:57](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/33911710))
* Background fibrovascular stroma
