Microscopic (histologic) description
  • Architecture
    • Ill defined, located in the papillary dermis, superficial reticular dermis and around follicles (Am J Surg Pathol 2001;25:696, J Cutan Pathol 2009;36:937)
    • Plaque-like growth pattern (Am J Surg Pathol 2001;25:696)
  • Cytological features
    • Multiple enlarged peripheral nerve fibers surrounded by mature squamous epithelium (Arch Toxicol 2011;85:1441)
    • Nerve bundles contain axons, Schwann cells and perineural cells (Am J Surg Pathol 2001;25:696)
    • Squamous sheath contains mature squamous epithelium and may have dyskeratotic keratinocytes
    • Keratinocytes typically do not show atypia, pleomorphism or mitoses (Clin Neuropathol 2020;39:232)
    • Neuroepithelial bundles may be surrounded by myxoid stroma and lymphocytic infiltrates
