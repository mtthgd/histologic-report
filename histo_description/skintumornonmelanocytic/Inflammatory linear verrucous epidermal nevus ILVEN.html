Microscopic (histologic) description
  • Skin biopsy shows psoriasiform epidermal hyperplasia with papillomatosis and hyperorthokeratosis alternating with parakeratosis (Pediatr Dermatol 1985;3:15)
  • Hypergranulosis is seen in areas of hyperorthokeratois; granular layer may be diminished in the areas of parakeratosis (Pediatr Dermatol 1985;3:15)
  • Variable inflammatory infiltrate may be seen (Pediatr Dermatol 1985;3:15)
  • Cases with spongiotic changes have been described in ILVEN (Dermatology 2006;212:103)
