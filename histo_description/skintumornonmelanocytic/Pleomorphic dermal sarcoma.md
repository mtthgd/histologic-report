# Pleomorphic dermal sarcoma

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Dermal based lesion composed of pleomorphic cells with vesicular nuclei and prominent nucleoli ([Cancer 1973;31:1541](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/4709969))
  + Cells can be spindled or epithelioid, often with admixed multinucleated giant cells
  + Cells can be arranged in sheets and fascicles
* Necrosis often present
* Perineural and lymphovascular invasion can be seen
* Infiltration into subcutis, skeletal muscle or fascia
* Additional findings include myxoid change, pseudoangiomatous growth and storiform growth ([Am J Surg Pathol 2012;36:1317](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/22510760))
