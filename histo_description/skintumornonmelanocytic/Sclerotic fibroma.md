# Sclerotic fibroma

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Unencapsulated dermal nodule
* Attenuated overlying epidermis
* Starry night or plywood appearance of whorled thickened collagen bundles ([J Cutan Pathol 2008;35:74](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/18544068))
* Low cellularity
* Absent elastic fibers
* Stromal mucin may be present
