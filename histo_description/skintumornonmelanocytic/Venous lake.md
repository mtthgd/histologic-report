# Venous lake

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Dilated vessel(s)
* Single layer of endothelium
* Typically no elastin or smooth muscle
* With or without thrombus
* Background solar elastosis (due to tendency to arise in sun damaged sites) ([J Am Acad Dermatol 1997;37:523](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/9344191))
