# Inflammatory fibroid polyp

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Submucosal mixture of prominent capillaries, spindle cells (occasionally concentrically arranged around vessels / onion skinning) and inflammatory cells (particularly eosinophils, plasma cells and mast cells) in granulation tissue-like stroma ([Arch Pathol Lab Med 2010;134:378](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/20196666))
* May be transmural
* May have lymphoid follicles
* Rare multinucleated giant cells, no atypia, no / rare mitotic figures
* **Histologic patterns:** classical, edematous, fibrovascular, nodular, sclerotic
