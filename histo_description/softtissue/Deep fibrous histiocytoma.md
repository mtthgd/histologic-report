# Deep fibrous histiocytoma

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Deep fibrous histiocytomas are well circumscribed
* Nodular cellular proliferation with collagen trapping ([Weiss: Enzinger and Weiss's Soft Tissue Tumors, 7th Edition, 2019](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/032361096X/pathologyoutl-20))
* Cells have minimal pleomorphism and atypia with no mitotic activity ([Weiss: Enzinger and Weiss's Soft Tissue Tumors, 7th Edition, 2019](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/032361096X/pathologyoutl-20))
* Tumor cells are spindled in shape with plump ovoid vesicular nuclei and pale eosinophilic cytoplasm, bland nuclei with vesicular chromatin and small single nucleoli ([Am J Surg Pathol 2008;32:354](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/18300816))
* Consists of monomorphic spindle cells in short intersecting fascicles arranged in a storiform pattern ([Weiss: Enzinger and Weiss's Soft Tissue Tumors, 7th Edition, 2019](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/032361096X/pathologyoutl-20), [Am J Surg Pathol 2008;32:354](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/18300816))
* Branching, hemangiopericytoma-like vascular pattern can be seen ([Am J Surg Pathol 2008;32:354](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/18300816))
* Touton type giant cells with phagocytosed lipid and hemosiderin may be seen; however, nearly half of all deep fibrous histiocytomas are cytologically monomorphic, lacking the foamy histiocytes and giant cells that are often seen in cutaneous lesions ([Weiss: Enzinger and Weiss's Soft Tissue Tumors, 7th Edition, 2019](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/032361096X/pathologyoutl-20), [Am J Surg Pathol 2008;32:354](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/18300816))
* Lymphocytes and a few xanthoma cells are dispersed throughout the lesion ([Weiss: Enzinger and Weiss's Soft Tissue Tumors, 7th Edition, 2019](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/032361096X/pathologyoutl-20))
* Hemorrhage, myxoid changes, cystic degeneration, central infarction is less frequent
* Interstitial or perivascular hyalinization is seen ([Weiss: Enzinger and Weiss's Soft Tissue Tumors, 7th Edition, 2019](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/032361096X/pathologyoutl-20), [Am J Surg Pathol 2008;32:354](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/18300816))
* Median mitosis of 3/10 high power fields (HPF) is seen; lymphovascular invasion and tumor necrosis are rare ([Am J Surg Pathol 2008;32:354](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/18300816))
* Combined pericytic and fibrous histiocytic areas are seen in fibrous histiocytoma of the orbit ([Weiss: Enzinger and Weiss's Soft Tissue Tumors, 7th Edition, 2019](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/032361096X/pathologyoutl-20))
* Degenerative fibrous histiocytoma has pleomorphic cells with hyperchromatic nuclei, which are called monster cells ([Weiss: Enzinger and Weiss's Soft Tissue Tumors, 7th Edition, 2019](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/032361096X/pathologyoutl-20))
* Pleomorphism and increased mitosis may indicate an aggressive lesion, atypical fibrous histiocytoma or a superficial pleomorphic sarcoma ([Weiss: Enzinger and Weiss's Soft Tissue Tumors, 7th Edition, 2019](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/032361096X/pathologyoutl-20))
* Despite having increased cellularity and mitotic activity, cellular fibrous histiocytoma is still considered benign ([Weiss: Enzinger and Weiss's Soft Tissue Tumors, 7th Edition, 2019](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/032361096X/pathologyoutl-20))
