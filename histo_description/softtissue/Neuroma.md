# Neuroma

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Circumscribed, unencapsulated spindle cell proliferation arranged in short bundles comprised of axons, Schwann cells, endoneurial cells and perineurial cells ([World J Clin Cases 2020;8:3821](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/32953859), [Acta Biomed 2020;91:122](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/32191665), [StatPearls: Neuroma [Accessed 21 September 2022]](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/31751028))
* Peripheral palisading is not present ([World J Clin Cases 2020;8:3821](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/32953859))
* Prominent scar tissue with dense collagen may be present ([Acta Biomed 2020;91:122](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/32191665), [StatPearls: Neuroma [Accessed 21 September 2022]](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/31751028))
* Dystrophic calcifications are rarely present ([Acta Biomed 2020;91:122](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/32191665))
