# Soft tissue chondroma

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Mature hyaline cartilage arranged in lobules with sharp borders
* Chondrocytes in lacunae, arranged diffusely or in small clusters
* Rarely can have moderate pleomorphism
* 33% show focal or diffuse calcifications
* Chondroblastoma-like soft tissue chondroma shows hypercellular areas with cells resembling chondroblasts, recently reclassified as calcified chondroid mesenchymal tumor ([Mod Pathol 2021;34:1373](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/33727696))
