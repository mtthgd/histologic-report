Microscopic (histologic) description
  • Triad of mature adipocytes, bland spindle cells and hyalinized rope-like collagen fibers
  • Multinucleated stromal giant cells and floret cells in pleomorphic lipoma
  • Myxoid background with mast cells frequent
  • Short and stubby spindle cells show occasional palisading
  • Rare lipoblasts present in half of cases (Hum Pathol 2017;65:140)
  • Infiltrative growth, necrosis, atypical spindle cells, pleomorphic lipoblasts and significant mitotic activity usually absent (Semin Diagn Pathol 2019;36:105)
  • Fat poor and fat free subtype: contains little (Am J Dermatopathol 2007;29:437, Am J Dermatopathol 2009;31:423)
  • Myxoid subtype: contains abundant myxoid stroma (Pathol Int 2014;64:346)
  • (Pseudo) angiomatous subtype: contains dilated endothelial lined channels and nodules of floating tumor (Histopathology 1994;24:565, Pathol Int 2007;57:26)
  • Plexiform variant has been described (Histopathology 1995;27:533)
  • Focal cartilaginous and osseous metaplasia rarely present (Histopathology 1987;11:803)
  • Extramedullary hematopoiesis may occur (Histopathology 2001;39:215)
