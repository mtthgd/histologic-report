# Leiomyosarcoma

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Composed of spindle cells with elongated nuclei and eosinophilic fibrillary cytoplasm arranged in fascicles
* Prominent atypia with enlarged, hyperchromatic and pleomorphic nuclei is seen with brisk mitotic activity and areas of necrosis ([Odze and Goldblum: Surgical Pathology of the GI Tract, Liver, Biliary tract, and Pancreas, 3rd Edition, 2015](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/1455707473/pathologyoutl-20))
