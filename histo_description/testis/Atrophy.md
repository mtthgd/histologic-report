# Atrophy

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Fibrovascular nodule with hemosiderin laden macrophages and dystrophic calcifications
* Rare presence of seminiferous tubules (6.3% of cases) and viable germ cells (2.1% of cases) in testicular remnants in testicular regression syndrome ([Front Pediatr 2022;10:834083](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/35433532))
* Small tubules, thick basement membrane and few or no germ cells in postvasectomy specimens
* Sclerotic tubules
* Interstitial fibrosis
* Increased Leydig cells
