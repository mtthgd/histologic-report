# Macrofollicular

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Frequent macrofollicles (> 50% of cross sectional area) and foci of follicular variant of papillary carcinoma, all with prominent, large ground glass nuclei and nuclear grooves ([Hum Pathol 1991;22:1195](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/1748427))
* Also cells with large but less pale nuclei with stippled chromatin, or cuboidal cells with hyperchromatic nuclei
