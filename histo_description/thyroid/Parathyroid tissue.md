# Parathyroid tissue

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Incidental intrathyroidal [PT tissue](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/parathyroidanatomy.html) / gland has a typical histology of PT
* Ectopic PT gland is completely encased by thyroid parenchyma
* [Adenoma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/parathyroidpthadenoma.html) may consist of any PT cell population and repeats the composition of orthotopic PT adenoma, with a lack of fatty tissue and peripheral rim of compressed PT parenchyma
* [Carcinoma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/parathyroidpthca.html) is diagnosed by the presence of invasion or metastasis
