# Histology of specimens from gender affirming surgery in individuals assigned female at birth

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Expanded endometrial stromal areas with decidua-like changes with or without glandular paucity ([Reprod Sci 2014;21:372](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/23885104), [Hum Reprod 1992;7:1461](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/1291578), [Int J Gynecol Pathol 2019;38:520](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/30252728))
* Ectocervical epithelial atrophy, often with transitional cell metaplasia ([Arch Pathol Lab Med 2022;146:742](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/34591101), [Surg Pathol Clin 2022;15:421](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/35715169), [Int J Gynecol Pathol 1997;16:89](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/9100060), [Int J Gynecol Pathol 2019;38:520](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/30252728))
  + Umbrella-like cells could be seen on the surface
  + Nuclei often show nuclear grooves
  + Koilocytotic atypia (with perinuclear halos) and viral cytopathic changes are distinctly absent
* Rarely, prostate type glands can be seen in the ectocervical epithelium ([Histopathology 2022;80:946](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/34664289), [Int J Gynecol Pathol 2017;36:328](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/27571240))
* Vaginal epithelial atrophy, transitional cell metaplasia and prostatic metaplasia ([Int J Gynecol Pathol 2004;23:182](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/15084849), [Am J Surg Pathol 2020;44:1040](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/32282346))
* Large ovarian cortical follicular cysts lined by nonluteinized granulosa cells ([Int J Gynecol Pathol 2019;38:520](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/30252728))
* Follicular density (primordial, intermediate and primary follicular count per mm2) is higher than expected for the given age ([Hormones (Athens) 2015;14:190](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/26158651), [Int J Gynecol Pathol 2019;38:520](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/30252728))
* Rarely, periadnexal virilized mesonephric duct remnants ([Int J Gynecol Pathol 2017;36:328](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/27571240))
