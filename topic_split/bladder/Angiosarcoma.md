Definition / general

* Rare vascular tumor with anastomosing vascular channels
* Endothelial cells usually exhibit marked cytologic atypia

Epidemiology

* Mean age 64 years, 89% occur in males

Etiology

* Due to radiation therapy for pelvic malignancy in 1/3; also exposure to arsenic, thorium dioxide, polyvinyl chloride or chemotherapeutic agents
* It has been reported to arise in bladder with preexisting hemangioma

Clinical features

* Patients typically present with hematuria
* Traditionally considered to have a poor prognosis, but see ([Arch Pathol Lab Med 2006;130:1543](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/17090199))

Case reports

* Survival 6 years after initial presentation ([Urology 2007;69:575.e9](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/17382176))

Treatment

* Wide radical resection, possibly radiotherapy/chemotherapy

Gross description

* Hemorrhagic, raised mass (mean 7 cm) of trigone or dome

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Anastomosing vascular channels lined by plump hyperchromatic cells
* Also solid growth and epithelioid cytology
* Typically invades the muscularis propria ([Am J Surg Pathol 2008;32:1213](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/18580491))

Positive stains

* [CD31](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/cdmarkerscd31.html), [CD34](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/cdmarkerscd34.html)
* [Cytokeratin](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsckgeneral.html) can be focally positive, especially in epithelioid tumors

Differential diagnosis

* [Hemangioma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/kidneytumorhemangiomas.html): typically 1 cm or less, 80% are cavernous, no atypia, no anastomosing or solid areas
* [Kaposi’s sarcoma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/softtissuekaposi.html): associated with HIV and HHV8; extravasated red blood cells present; usually less atypia than angiosarcoma
* [Sarcomatoid urothelial carcinoma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/bladdersarcomatoidvariant.html): undifferentiated spindle cells; associated urothelial carcinoma; vascular markers are negative
