Definition / general

* Undifferentiated round cell sarcoma characterized by *BCOR::CCNB3* rearrangement (previously Ewing-like sarcoma)

Essential features

* Predominantly arises from the bones of children and young adult males
* Sheets or fascicles of round and spindle cells forming whorls and hemangiopericytic patterns, usually within a myxoid background
* Nuclear CCNB3 and BCOR positivity as well as TLE1 and SATB2 coexpression
* Molecular diagnosis is highly recommended: *BCOR::CCNB3*
* Similar treatment and comparable prognosis with [Ewing sarcoma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/boneewing.html)

Terminology

* *BCOR::CCNB3* positive sarcoma
* Undifferentiated round cell sarcoma with *BCOR::CCNB3* fusion

Epidemiology

* Children and young adult males (mean age is 15; M:F = 3.1) ([Am J Surg Pathol 2018;42:604](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/29300189), [Nat Genet 2012;44:461](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/22387997), [Int J Surg Pathol 2023 Jan 2 [Epub ahead of print]](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/36591870))
* Accounts for 4% of undifferentiated round cell sarcomas ([Surg Pathol Clin 2017;10:587](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/28797504))

Sites

* Bone > soft tissues > viscera (e.g., kidney) ([Am J Surg Pathol 2018;42:604](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/29300189), [Nat Genet 2012;44:461](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/22387997))
* Mostly long bones > pelvis > vertebral column

Pathophysiology

* Both *CCNB3* itself and *BCOR::CCNB3* as a full fusion transcript seem to play a role in oncogenesis and phenotype ([Nat Genet 2012;44:461](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/22387997))

Etiology

* Unknown

Clinical features

* Mainly metastasizes to the lung

Diagnosis

* Core needle biopsy
* Confirmatory demonstration of *BCOR::CCNB3* fusion by FISH, PCR or massive parallel sequencing (MPS)
* Reference: [Int J Surg Pathol 2023 Jan 2 [Epub ahead of print]](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/36591870)

Radiology description

* Permeative lytic / sclerotic tumor invariably associated with cortical thickening in the metadiaphyseal region of long bones
* Wide zone of transition with periosteal reaction
* MRI: iso or hypointense on T1 and heterogeneously hyperintense on T2 with postcontrast enhancement
* PET CT: mean SUVmax = 6.3 (range: 5.7 - 6.9)
* Reference: [Skeletal Radiol 2021;50:521](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/32840647)

Prognostic factors

* Prognosis is comparable to [Ewing sarcoma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/boneewing.html) and less aggressive than [*CIC-DUX4* sarcoma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/boneCICDUX4.html)
* 5 year overall survival is 75%; disease free survival is 70% ([Pediatr Blood Cancer 2014;61:2191](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/25176412), [Am J Surg Pathol 2018;42:604](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/29300189))

Case reports

* 1 year old boy with refractory pediatric *BCOR::CCNB3* sarcoma with complete response to palbociclib ([JCO Precis Oncol 2020;4:PO.19.00258](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/32923894))
* 11 and 12 year old boys with primary renal sarcomas with *BCOR::CCNB3* fusion ([Am J Surg Pathol 2017;41:1702](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/28817404))
* 18 year old man with a primary renal sarcoma ([Int J Surg Pathol 2021;29:194](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/32648496))

Treatment

* Similar to [Ewing sarcoma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/boneewing.html)
* Neoadjuvant chemotherapy with Ewing regimen followed by surgery or radiation for local control ([Pediatr Blood Cancer 2014;61:2191](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/25176412))

Gross description

* 5 - 15 cm (median of 10 cm)
* Well circumscribed, gray-cream colored, soft and fleshy, solid, homogeneous mass with necrotic foci
* Metadiaphysis of long bones with soft tissue involvement
* Reference: [Int J Surg Pathol 2023 Jan 2 [Epub ahead of print]](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/36591870)

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Round or spindle cells arranged in sheets or fascicles
* Angulated and hyperchromatic nuclei with fine chromatin and inconspicuous nucleoli
* Wide range of mitosis (5 - 60/10 high power fields, median of 30) ([Pediatr Blood Cancer 2014;61:2191](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/25176412))
* Small foci of necrosis (50 - 90%)
* Significant variation in cellularity and morphological patterns, usually with an abrupt transition:
  + Small clusters of epithelioid cells ([Virchows Arch 2017;470:373](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/28197724))
  + Nests and cords of tumor cells
  + Myxoid background (50%) ([Am J Surg Pathol 2018;42:604](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/29300189))
  + Collagenous stroma (36%) ([Am J Surg Pathol 2018;42:604](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/29300189))
  + Hemangiopericytic pattern
  + Whorling pattern
  + Hemorrhagic / telangiectatic areas
* References: [Am J Surg Pathol 2017;41:1713](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/28877060), [Am J Surg Pathol 2014;38:1307](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/24805859), [Am J Surg Pathol 2018;42:604](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/29300189), [Nat Genet 2012;44:461](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/22387997), [Pediatr Blood Cancer 2014;61:2191](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/25176412), [Indian J Med Res 2019;150:557](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/32048619), [Virchows Arch 2017;470:373](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/28197724), [Mod Pathol 2015;28:575](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/25360585)

Cytology description

* Round, spindle and rhabdoid-like cells
* Double cell population similar to [Ewing sarcoma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/boneewing.html) (larger, lighter cells and smaller, darker cells)
* Small clusters with thin and delicate vascular cores (in contrast to thicker vascular cores in Ewing sarcoma and [synovial sarcoma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/softtissuesynovialsarc.html))
* References: [Cytopathology 2021;32:771](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/34265123), [Am J Clin Pathol 2020;153:315](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/31679010)

Molecular / cytogenetics description

* Accounts for 60% of *BCOR* gene alterations (*BCOR* internal tandem duplication [ITD]); other partner genes are *MAML3* and *ZC3H7B*
* Paracentric inversion on chromosome X resulting in recurrent gene fusion of *BCOR* (encoding BCL6 interacting corepressor) on Xp11.4 and *CCNB3* (cyclin B3) on Xp11.22
* Gene profiling and single nucleotide polymorphism (SNP) analyses resulted in a distinct cluster from small round cell sarcomas, including Ewing sarcoma ([Nat Genet 2012;44:461](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/22387997))

*BCOR::CCNB3* sarcoma of the proximal tibia

Sample pathology report

* Proximal metadiaphyseal region of the right tibia, incisional biopsy:
  + *BCOR::CCNB3* sarcoma (see comment)
  + Comment: Undifferentiated round and spindle neoplastic cells showed dot-like CD99 staining as well as TLE1 and SATB2 coexpression. BCOR and CCNB3 were diffusely positive. Breakapart FISH was negative for *EWSR1* or *SYT* rearrangements. On reverse transcription PCR, *BCOR::CCNB3* fusion product / band was observed at 140 bp.

Differential diagnosis

* **Mainly other small round cell tumors**
  + [Ewing sarcoma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/boneewing.html):
    - No spindling or myxoid background
    - [CD99](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/cdmarkerscd99.html) diffuse strong membranous in Ewing sarcoma versus variable (usually dot-like) in *BCOR::CCNB3* sarcoma
    - [BCOR](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsbcor.html) and [NKX2.2](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsNKX2.html) may help but consider limitations
    - [SATB2](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainssatb2.html) is only rarely positive
    - **CCNB3**-, [TLE1](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainstle1.html)-
    - *FET-ETS* fusion
  + [*CIC-DUX4* sarcoma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/boneCICDUX4.html):
    - Median age is higher
    - Soft tissue > bone
    - Sheets separated with septi
    - Prominent nucleoli
    - [WT1](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainswt1.html)+, **ETV4**+
    - *CIC-DUX4* fusion
  + [Small cell osteosarcoma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/boneosteosarcomageneral.html):
    - [CD99](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/cdmarkerscd99.html)-
      * Small cell osteosarcoma can also be positive for [CD99](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/cdmarkerscd99.html), so positive staining with this marker is not helpful ([J Bone Oncol 2020;24:100305](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/32775179))
    - Osteoid production may be seen in both
    - Lacks specific gene fusion
  + [Synovial sarcoma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/softtissuesynovialsarc.html):
    - Rare in bone
    - Both synovial sarcoma and *BCOR::CCNB3* sarcomas are [TLE1](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainstle1.html)+
    - [EMA](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsema.html) can be focally positive in *BCOR::CCNB3* sarcoma but [pankeratin](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsae1ae3.html) is negative
    - *SS18-SSX* fusion specific antibody and SSX C terminus positive
    - *SYT-SSX* fusion
  + [Rhabdomyosarcoma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/softtissueembryonalrhabdo.html):
    - Rare in bone
    - Myogenic markers ([desmin](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsdesmin.html), [myogenin](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsmyogenin.html), [MyoD1](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsmyod1.html))+
  + [Sclerosing epithelioid fibrosarcoma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/softtissuesclerosingepithelioidfibrosarcoma.html) / [low grade fibromyxoid tumor](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/softtissuelgfibromyxoid.html):
    - Rare in bone
    - Cords of small to epithelioid cells in collagenous background
    - [MUC4](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsmuc2.html#muc4)+
    - *EWSR1-CREBL1/2* fusion
  + [Malignant peripheral nerve sheath tumor](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/softtissuempnst.html):
    - Rare in bone
    - **H2K27me2** / [3](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsh3k27me3.html) loss
    - [S100](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainss100.html) or [SOX10](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainssox10.html) positive in a subset
  + [Neuroblastoma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/adrenalneuroblastoma.html) ([metastasis](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/bonemetstobone.html)):
    - [CD99](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/cdmarkerscd99.html)-
    - **PHOX12B**+, [synaptophysin](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainssynaptophysin.html)+
    - Lacks *BCOR* alteration
