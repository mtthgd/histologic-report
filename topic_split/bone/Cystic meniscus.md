Definition / general

* Lesion similar to ganglion but in menisci of knee
* Either at lateral or medial joint margin ([AJR Am J Roentgenol 2001;177:409](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/11461872))
* Either confined to meniscus or extends beyond capsule
* Almost always associated with meniscal tears; may be due to trauma
* Cyst caused by simple extrusion of synovial fluid through meniscal tear
* Usually pain and swelling

Case reports

* 27 year old man with symptomatic posterior cruciate ganglion cyst ([Knee Surg Relat Res 2012;24:52](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/22570853))
* 66 year old woman with tumor-like medial meniscal cyst ([Clinics (Sao Paulo) 2008;63:411](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/18568256))
