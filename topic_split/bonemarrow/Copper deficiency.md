Definition / general

* Rare cause of sideroblastic anemia and neutropenia ([Am J Hematol 2007;82:625](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/17236184))
* Often due to excess zinc ingestion ([CMAJ 2003;169:129](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/12874162), [Am J Clin Pathol 2005;123:125](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/15762288))
* Also due to zinc inborn errors of metabolism ([J Pediatr Hematol Oncol 2005;27:477](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/16189440), [Arch Neurol 2003;60:1303](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/12975299)) and total parenteral nutrition ([Rinsho Ketsueki 1993;34:171](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/8492415))

Treatment

* Oral copper gluconate

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Resembles myelodysplasia or sideroblastic anemia
* Presence of cytoplasmic vacuoles is suggestive ([Ann Clin Biochem 2005;42:227](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/15949160), [Am J Clin Pathol 1992;97:665](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/1575212))
