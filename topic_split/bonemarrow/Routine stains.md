Definition / general

* Note: reactivity varies by type of fixation
* [CD45 / LCA](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/cdmarkerscd45.html): lymphocytes
* [Kappa](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainskappa.html) and [lambda light chains](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainskappa.html): to determine clonality or not of lymphocytes and plasma cells
* [Myeloperoxidase](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsmyeloperoxidase.html) and [lysozyme](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainslysozyme.html): for AML subtyping
* B cell markers: [CD20](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/cdmarkerscd20.html), [CD79a](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/cdmarkerscd79a.html) and possibly [PAX5](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainspax5.html)
* T cell marker: [CD3](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/cdmarkerscd3.html)
* [TdT](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainstdt.html): for ALL
* [Iron stains](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsiron.html) (Prussian blue): on aspirate smears and trephine biopsies ([J Clin Pathol 2005;58:269](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/15735158))
  + 0 = Absent
  + 1 = Trace
  + 2 = Present (sparse)
  + 3 = Present (moderate)
  + 4 = Abundant (abnormal)
  + To assess iron stores, examine 7 - 9 particles ([J Clin Pathol 2004;57:1038](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/15452156))
  + Absence of staining iron is not diagnostic of iron deficiency anemia ([Ann Hematol 2001;80:166](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/11320902))
* Possibly [reticulin stain](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsreticulin.html) or [Masson trichrome stain](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainstrichrome.html)
* Also [CD34](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/cdmarkerscd34.html) (blasts and blood vessels), [Factor VIII](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsfactorviii.html) (blood vessels), [CD61](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/cdmarkerscd61.html) (megakaryocytes), [CD68](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/cdmarkerscd68.html) (macrophages), Hemoglobin A (for AML-M6) and [glycophorin](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/cdmarkerscd230.html#cd235) (for erythroid cells)
