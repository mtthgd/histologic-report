Definition / general

* Defined as Up to 10% of AML cases
* Median age 67 years ([Leuk Res 1996;20:563](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/8795690))
* Rare blasts in peripheral blood
* Often has smoldering course although intensive chemotherapy may cause complete remission

Case reports

* 78 year old man and woman with minimally differentiated hypoplastic leukemia ([Nihon Ronen Igakkai Zasshi 1997;34:70](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/9077109))

Differential diagnosis

* [Aplastic anemia](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/bonemarrowaplasticanemia.html): no excess blasts, interstitial bone marrow cells are plasma cells, lymphocytes and mast cells, not blasts
* [Refractory anemia with excess blasts](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/myeloproliferativeRAEB.html)
* Substance abuse: see [Arch Pathol Lab Med 2005;129:e35](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/15679445)
