Definition / general

* 10% of AML cases; 5% of childhood leukemias
* Any age, 20% are Anemia, thrombocytopenia, neutropenia; variable number of blasts in peripheral blood
* Variable prognosis
* **Criteria for diagnosis:** 20%+ nonerythroid cells in peripheral blood or bone marrow are myeloblasts; monocytic precursors are **Enzyme cytochemistry:** most blasts are positive for myeloperoxidase or Sudan Black B, and chloroacetate esterase

Case reports

* 7 year old boy with myeloid sarcoma with acute myeloblastic leukemia ([J Coll Physicians Surg Pak 2011;21:369](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/21711997))
* 30 year old man with t(5;11) ([Cancer Genet Cytogenet 2007;172:154](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/17213025))
* 35 year old pregnant woman ([Rinsho Ketsueki 2011;52:18](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/21378477))
* Three patients with variant t(8;21) ([Cancer Genet Cytogenet 2010;199:31](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/20417866))

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Usually hypercellular marrow
* Full range of myeloid maturation through maturing neutrophils, often with abnormal segmentation and 10%+ bone marrow cells with variable degree of dysplasia
* Auer rods in 70% of blasts; myeloblasts with or without azurophilic granules
* Erythroid and megakaryocyte precursors may have dysplastic changes
* Often increased eosinophilic precursors without cytological and cytochemical abnormalities of inv(16)(p13.1q22)
* Basophils may be increased, rarely mast cell hyperplasia ([Indian J Pathol Microbiol 2007;50:655](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/17883174))

Molecular / cytogenetics description

* t(8;21) cases classified as [**AML with t(8;21); RUNX1-RUNX1T1**](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/leukemiaAMLwitht821q22q22.html); may be only genetic abnormality or part of more complex abnormalities ([Cytometry B Clin Cytom 2008;74:25](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/18061959))
* FLT3 mutations associated with HLA-DR negative patients ([Leuk Res 2007;31:921](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/17056111))
