Definition / general

* Not part of WHO classification
* Frequent in acute promyelocytic leukemia; up to 25% of AML-M2 ([Acta Paediatr Jpn 1990;32:651](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/2082666))
* Giant granules may be due to fusion of primary granules or small dense vesicles
* See also [Bone marrow nonneoplastic](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/bonemarrow.html#ChediakHigashiSyndrome) chapter
