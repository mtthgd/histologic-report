# t514q31q32 iIGHIL3i

t(5;14)(q31;q32); *IGH::IL3*

Definition / general

* Diagnosis based on immunophenotype and genetic findings, even with low blast count
* Very rare; Blood 1989;73:2081)
* Presents with variable eosinophilia; may be asymptomatic and lack any blasts ([Medicine (Baltimore) 1990;69:232](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/2374508))

Case reports

* 13 year old boy with cardiac disease ([BMJ Case Rep 2012 Jan 20;2012](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/22665861))
* CD10+ acute leukemia with t(5q;14q) translocation and hypereosinophilia ([Acta Haematol 1989;82:85](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/2508400))

Microscopic (histologic) description

* CD19+ CD10+ lymphoblasts with circulating reactive eosinophils

Molecular / cytogenetics description

* t(5;14)(q31;q32); IL3-IGH ([Atlas of Genetics and Cytogenetics in Oncology and Haematology: t(5;14)(q31;q32) IGH/IL3 [Accessed 12 April 2018]](http://atlasgeneticsoncology.org/Anomalies/t514ID1111.html))
