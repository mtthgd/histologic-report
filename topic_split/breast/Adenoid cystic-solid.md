Terminology

* Also called basaloid type

Clinical features

* Axillary nodal metastases in 2 of 6 cases ([Am J Surg Pathol 2002;26:413](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/11914618))
* May have poorer prognosis than classic type ([APMIS 1999;107:762](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/10515126))

Case reports

* Solid variant of mammary "adenoid cystic carcinoma with basaloid features" merging with "small cell carcinoma" ([Pathol Res Pract 2005;201:705](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/16325513))

Gross description

* Solitary mass, mean 4 cm
