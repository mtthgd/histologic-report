Definition / general

* Biphasic benign breast tumor with varying epithelial and stromal components

Essential features

* Similar to pleomorphic adenoma of the [salivary gland](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/salivaryglandspleomorphicadenoma.html) in morphology, immunohistochemistry, prognosis and treatment, except rarer in breast
* Hypothesized to be a variant of [intraductal papilloma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/breastpapilloma.html)
* Biphasic benign tumor easily misdiagnosed as mucinous or matrix producing [metaplastic carcinoma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/breastmalignantmetaplastic.html)

Terminology

* Also called benign mixed tumor, [chondroid syringoma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/skintumornonmelanocyticchondroidsyringoma.html) (in skin)

ICD coding

* ICD-11: [2F30.Y](https://icd.who.int/browse11/l-m/en#/http%3a%2f%2fid.who.int%2ficd%2fentity%2f2120366482%2fmms%2fother) - Other specified benign neoplasm of breast
* ICD-11: [XH2KC1](https://icd.who.int/browse11/l-m/en#/http%3a%2f%2fid.who.int%2ficd%2fentity%2f1856218901) - Pleomorphic adenoma

Epidemiology

* 1906 - current: < 100 case reports ([Histopathology 2016;68:45](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/26348644))

Sites

* Periareolar, upper outer quadrant

Pathophysiology

* Breast and salivary glands are embryologically similar exocrine glands that originate from the same ectodermal layer and can differentiate towards dual epithelial myoepithelial cell differentiation

Etiology

* May derive from divergent differentiation of a single pluripotent stem cell resulting in epithelial myoepithelial cell differentiation and stroma ([Hum Pathol 1991;22:1206](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/1660850))
* Periareolar location:
  + Large ducts with numerous myoepithelial cells ([Clin Oncol 1982;8:361](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/6301730))
  + Arise in association with [intraductal papilloma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/breastpapilloma.html) and therefore may be the same entity ([Am J Surg Pathol 1990;14:913](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/1698339))

Clinical features

* Age range: 19 - 85 years
* F > M, only 3 case reports in men ([J Clin Pathol 2003;56:497](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/12835294))

Diagnosis

* Diagnosis may be made on core biopsy or FNA with radiologic correlation but definitive with excision

Radiology description

* Mammogram: circumscribed round lesion simulating [fibroadenoma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/breastfibroadenoma.html), variable calcifications ([Case Rep Pathol 2015;2015:172750](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/25830053))
* Ultrasound: homogeneously smooth lobulated internal mass or complex cystic mass, resembling [intraductal papilloma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/breastpapilloma.html)
* Tomography: isointense mass with internal septations

Prognostic factors

* Low grade indolent tumor
* Recurrences (3 case reports) due to inadequate margins or multifocal tumors ([Hum Pathol 1991;22:1206](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/1660850), [Cancer 1988;61:997](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/2827885), [Breast J 2007;13:418](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/17593049))

Case reports

* 55 year old woman with subareolar breast mass ([Pathol Res Pract 2005;201:333](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/15991841))
* 57 and 78 year old women with breast masses ([BMJ Case Rep 2015;2015:bcr2015210906](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/26123472))
* 59 year old woman with pleomorphic adenoma of the breast ([Arch Pathol Lab Med 2003;127:474](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/12683878))
* 70 year old woman with 2 cm mass, electron microscopy study ([Am J Clin Pathol 1990;93:795](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/2161180))
* 71 year old woman with fine needle aspiration of 23 mm breast mass ([Diagn Cytopathol 2018;46:56](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/28994509))

Treatment

* Wide excision with clear margins (2 - 5 mm) ([Breast J 2007;13:418](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/17593049))

Gross description

* Circumscribed yellow white gritty solid nodules > polypoid, multinodular or satellite lesions
* Size: typically 0.8 - 4.5 cm (mean = 2.0 cm) ([South Med J 1975;68:97](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/168643))
* Intraductal nodule with cystification

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Admixture of epithelial and myoepithelial cells embedded in a chondromyxoid stroma, may show chondroid or osseous metaplasia
* Architecture includes tubules, islands, cords, trabeculae, sheets or pseudoglandular structures lined by 2 cell types
  + Inner epithelial cells: cuboidal or columnar epithelial cells, with or without apocrine or squamous metaplasia
  + Outer myoepithelial cells: polygonal, plasmacytoid, fusiform or stellate with clear to eosinophilic cytoplasm
* No atypia, mitoses, necrosis or infiltrative growth
* Rare malignant transformation
  + Carcinoma ex pleomorphic adenoma = 3 cases ([Virchows Arch 2005;446:142](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/15583933))
  + Epithelial myoepithelial carcinoma ex pleomorphic adenoma = 1 case ([Med J Armed Forces India 2011;67:74](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/27365768))

Myxoid stromal breast tumor

Cytology description

* Cellular smears consisting of epithelial and myoepithelial cell clusters and sheets arranged in branched glandular structures
  + Epithelial cells: round cells with abundant cytoplasm
  + Myoepithelial cells: stellate, plasmacytoid
* Stroma: myxochondroid, light green on Pap, metachromatic (red-purple) on Giemsa
* No atypia, mitoses or necrosis
* Pitfall for misdiagnosis as mucinous or matrix producing [metaplastic carcinoma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/breastmalignantmetaplastic.html) ([Breast Dis 2017;37:105](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/28655120))

Positive stains

* [Alcian blue](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsalcianblue.html) hyaluronidase sensitive ([Arch Pathol Lab Med 2003;127:474](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/12683878))
* Epithelial cells: [AE1 / AE3](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsae1ae3.html), [CK5/6](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsck5and6.html), [CK14](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsck14.html), [CK 34 beta E12](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsck34be12.html), [CEA](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainscea.html), [EMA](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsema.html), [vimentin](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsvimentin.html), [GFAP](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsgfap.html), [GCDFP-15](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsgcdfp15.html), variable [ER](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainser.html)
* Myoepithelial cells: [CK14](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsck14.html), [vimentin](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsvimentin.html), [S100](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainss100.html), [actin](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsactin.html), [calponin](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainscalponin.html), [p63](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsp63.html), [GFAP](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsgfap.html), **HMGIC, HGMIY**

Negative stains

* [Mucin](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsmucins.html) [PASD](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainspas.html)

Electron microscopy description

* Biphasic tumor ([Am J Clin Pathol 1990;93:795](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/2161180))
  + Epithelial: microvilli and lumina
  + Myoepithelial: spindled stromal cells with intermediate filaments, dense bodies and intercellular junctions

Molecular / cytogenetics description

* Diploid
* Deletion of *PLAG1* by FISH
* *HMGIC* and *HGMIY* not rearranged by FISH ([Pathol Res Pract 2005;201:333](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/15991841))
* Similar to ear, nose and throat sites, *HMGA2-WIFI* and *CTNNB1-PLAG1* have been identified in case reports ([NPJ Breast Cancer 2020;6:20](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/32550265))

Sample pathology report

* Left breast, mass, lumpectomy:
  + Pleomorphic adenoma, 3.2 cm (see comment)
  + Surgical margins, negative for tumor
  + Comment: The lesion is a circumscribed benign biphasic tumor by morphology and immunohistochemistry. AE1/3 and p63 are expressed. There is associated myxoid stroma. These findings are consistent with pleomorphic adenoma.

Differential diagnosis

* [Intraductal papilloma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/breastpapilloma.html) with chondromyxoid stroma:
  + Chondromyxoid stroma is focal, has uniform composition and is a minor component
* [Metaplastic carcinoma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/breastmalignantmetaplastic.html), **matrix producing**:
  + May be difficult to differentiate on a core needle biopsy
  + Infiltrative tumor, has atypia, mitoses, necrosis
* [Ductal adenoma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/breastductaladenoma.html):
  + Lacks myxoid stroma
  + Intraductal / within duct lumen
  + May be a variant of [intraductal papilloma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/breastpapilloma.html)
* [Mucinous carcinoma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/breastmalignantmucinous.html):
  + Infiltrative tumor, has atypia, mitoses, mucin
  + With or without [ductal carcinoma in situ](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/breastmalignantDCIS.html)
  + [Alcian blue](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsalcianblue.html) hyaluronidase resistant ([Arch Pathol Lab Med 2003;127:474](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/12683878))
* [Adenomyoepithelioma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/breastadenomyo.html):
  + Lacks chondromyxoid stroma
* [Phyllodes tumor](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/breastphyllodesgeneral.html) with chondromyxoid differentiation:
  + Leafy architecture with cellular stroma
  + Stroma with mitotic figures
  + Chondromyxoid areas with atypia
