Case reports

* Patient with melanosis of uterine cervix after cryotherapy for epithelial dysplasia ([Am J Clin Pathol 1990;93:802](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/2189292))

Gross description

* Flat, dark lesion up to 3 cm

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Benign pigmented melanocytes in basal layer of epithelium
* No thickening of epithelium
* Melanocytes are densely pigmented and dendritic but do not involve the stroma

Differential diagnosis

* [Blue nevus](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/cervixbluenevus.html)
