Definition / general

* Rare, benign, polypoid lesion of cervix or vagina of young girls to adult women
* Treat with local excision; may recur, but good prognosis

Terminology

* Mesonephric papilloma may be similar entity ([Geburtshilfe Frauenheilkd 1986;46:654](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/3770416), [J Pediatr 1963;63:211](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/14043062))

Case reports

* 18 month girl with Müllerian papilloma and multiple renal cysts ([Urology 2005;65:388](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/15708065))
* 4 year old girl with recurrent cervical tumor ([J Pediatr Adolesc Gynecol 1998;11:29](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/9526823))
* 49 year old woman with borderline malignant change in vaginal tumor ([J Clin Pathol 1998;51:875](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/10193336))

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Superficially located, composed of papillary stalks covered by mucinous epithelium with focal squamous metaplasia
* Stroma is highly cellular fibrous tissue
* No atypia, minimal mitotic activity
