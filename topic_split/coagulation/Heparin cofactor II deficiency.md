Definition / general

* Very rare hypercoagulable condition, either hereditary (autosomal dominant, 15 families documented through 2002) or acquired (liver disease)
* Associated with thrombosis, but not a strong risk factor by itself
* Testing patients with thromboembolic disease for heparin cofactor II deficiency is not recommended as a first or second line test ([Arch Pathol Lab Med 2002;126:1394](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/12421148))

Case reports

* Family with homozygous deficiency, but no significant symptoms ([Circulation 2004;110:1303](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/15337701))
