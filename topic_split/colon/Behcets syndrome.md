Definition / general

* Multisystem inflammatory disorder of unknown origin, with aphthous stomatitis, genital ulcers, relapsing iritis; also arthritis, cutaneous and vascular disease ([eMedicine #1](http://emedicine.medscape.com/article/1006358-overview), [#2](http://emedicine.medscape.com/article/329099-overview))
* GI involvement in 10% (ileum, cecum); causes pain, diarrhea, melena; rarely perforation ([Surg Today 2003;33:383](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/12734737))

Epidemiology

* More common in region from Mediterranean to eastern Asia
* 2 / 3 in males

Case reports

* 16 year old girl with colonic Behcet's disease with pyoderma gangrenosum ([World J Gastroenterol 2006;12:979](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/16521233))
* 17 year old monozygotic male twins with concordant colonic Behçet's disease([J Gastroenterol 2005;40:421](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/15870978))
* 17 year old boy with colonic perforation in Behcet's syndrome
  ([World J Gastroenterol 2008;14:6578](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/19030217))
* 20 year old man with Behçet's disease with multiple colon perforations
  ([Turk J Gastroenterol 2010;21:324](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/20931444))
* 21 year old man with multiple perforations along the transverse colon
  ([Clinics (Sao Paulo) 2009;64:1231](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/20037714))
* 29 year old woman with severe colitis ([Am J Surg Pathol 1986;10:888](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/3789253))
* 34 year old man with case resembling inflammatory bowel disease ([Intern Med 2004;43:243](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/15098609))
* 37 year old woman with case with longitudinal ulcers and granulomas resembling Crohn's ([J Gastroenterol Hepatol 2002;17:105](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/11895563))
* 77 year old man with colon cancer arising in an ulcer scar due to intestinal Behçet's disease ([Intern Med 2011;50:429](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/21372452))

Treatment

* Steroids
