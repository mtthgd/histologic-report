Definition / general

* Vascular malignancy caused by human herpes virus 8 (see topic in [Soft tissue](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/softtissuekaposi.html) chapter)

Essential features

* Vascular malignancy most often seen in AIDS patients with HHV8 infection
* Rare in gastrointestinal tract

Epidemiology

* Most common gastrointestinal malignancy attributable to AIDS ([Am J Gastroenterol 1991;86:715](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/2038993))
* Currently rare; incidence decreased from use of HAART for AIDS treatment

Etiology

* HHV8 infection

Clinical features

* Usually no clinical sequelae ([Gastroenterology 1985;89:102](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/4007399))
* Rarely causes diarrhea, protein losing enteropathy or abdominal pain

Prognostic factors

* Degree of immunosuppression influences prognosis ([Scand J Gastroenterol 1991;26:1007](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/1947766))

Case reports

* 77 year old woman with extensive colonic disease that resolved after chemotherapy ([J Cancer Res Ther 2012;8:112](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/22531526))
* 87 year old man with coexisting colon cancer ([Acta Dermatovenerol Alp Panonica Adriat 2011;20:83](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/21993707))

Treatment

* Chemotherapy and radiation preferred over surgery

Kaposi sarcoma

Gross description

* Reddish brown to purple macules or polyps, typically 0.5 - 1.5 cm

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Lamina propria based lesion composed of spindle cells with mild to moderate atypia, arranged in vague fascicles and separated by slit-like vessels
* Extravasated red blood cells common
* PAS positive, diastase resistant hyaline globules sometimes seen

Positive stains

* [CD31](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/cdmarkerscd31.html), [CD34](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/cdmarkerscd34.html), [HHV8](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainshhv8.html)

Negative stains

* [S100](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainss100.html), [desmin](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsdesmin.html), [muscle specific actin](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsactinmusclespecific.html)

Sample pathology report

* Rectum, mass, biopsy:
  + Vascular proliferation most consistent with Kaposi sarcoma (see comment)
  + Comment: An immunostain for HHV8 is positive in the lesional cells.

Differential diagnosis

* [Angiosarcoma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/softtissueangiosarcoma.html):
  + Negative for [HHV8](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainshhv8.html); often epithelioid in gastrointestinal tract
* [Gastrointestinal stromal tumor](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/colontumorgist.html):
  + Positive for [CD34](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/cdmarkerscd34.html) but also for [DOG1](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsdog1.html); lesion is typically larger and not vascular
* [Melanoma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/skintumormelanocyticmelanoma.html):
  + Positive for [S100](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainss100.html)
