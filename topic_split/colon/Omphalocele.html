Omphalocele
Table of Contents
Definition / general | Case reports | Clinical images | Differential diagnosis
Definition / general
  • Infant born with herniated abdominal contents into ventral membranous sac through umbilical cord, due to failure of abdominal musculature to form (eMedicine: Pediatric Omphalocele and Gastroschisis [Accessed 16 July 2018], eMedicine: Omphalocele Imaging [Accessed 16 July 2018])
  • May be related to ectopically induced Hedgehog signaling (PLoS One 2011;6:e16260)
Case reports
  • Newborn with omphalocele and gastroschisis associated with multiple congenital abnormalities (J Med Life 2011;4:295)
  • Infant with colonic atresia and Hirschsprung disease (Pediatr Surg Int 2001;17:218)
