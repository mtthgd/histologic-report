Definition / general

* Infant born with herniated abdominal contents into ventral membranous sac through umbilical cord, due to failure of abdominal musculature to form ([eMedicine: Pediatric Omphalocele and Gastroschisis [Accessed 16 July 2018]](http://emedicine.medscape.com/article/975583-overview), [eMedicine: Omphalocele Imaging [Accessed 16 July 2018]](http://emedicine.medscape.com/article/404182-overview))
* May be related to ectopically induced Hedgehog signaling ([PLoS One 2011;6:e16260](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/21283718))

Case reports

* Newborn with omphalocele and gastroschisis associated with multiple congenital abnormalities ([J Med Life 2011;4:295](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/22567055))
* Infant with colonic atresia and Hirschsprung disease ([Pediatr Surg Int 2001;17:218](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/11315294))
