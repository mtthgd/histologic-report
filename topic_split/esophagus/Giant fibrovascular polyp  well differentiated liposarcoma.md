Definition / general

* Giant fibrovascular polyp used to be considered a nonneoplastic polypoid lesion of the esophagus
* The majority of giant fibrovascular polyps of the esophagus now appear to represent well differentiated and dedifferentiated liposarcoma, based on the result of *MDM2* FISH (fluorescence in situ hybridization) testing ([Mod Pathol 2018;31:337](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/28984298))

Essential features

* Pedunculated polypoid (sausage shaped) mass, up to 23 cm in length (average 13 cm) ([World J Gastrointest Oncol 2016;8:835](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/28035254))
* Consists of fibroadipose tissue with slightly enlarged, hyperchromatic stromal cells ([Mod Pathol 2018;31:337](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/28984298))
* MDM2 and CDK4 overexpression by immunohistochemistry and amplification by FISH, indicating diagnosis of liposarcoma ([Mod Pathol 2018;31:337](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/28984298))

Terminology

* Giant fibrovascular polyp, fibrovascular polyp, well differentiated liposarcoma, atypical lipomatous tumor

Epidemiology

* Approximately 0.5% of all esophageal neoplasms ([World J Gastrointest Oncol 2016;8:835](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/28035254))
* Middle aged to elderly patients with male predominance

Sites

* Proximal / cervical esophagus ([Int J Surg Case Rep 2019;64:113](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/31630086))

Clinical features

* Middle aged to elderly patients with male predominance ([Int J Surg Case Rep 2019;64:113](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/31630086))
* Slowly worsening dysphagia, weight loss, odynophagia and oral regurgitation of polyp

Diagnosis

* Characteristic radiological and endoscopic appearances, plus characteristic histologic findings with immunohistochemical / molecular confirmation

Prognostic factors

* Dedifferentiated type tends to recur and metastasize ([Mod Pathol 2018;31:337](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/28984298))

Case reports

* 36 year old woman with an 11 cm giant fibrovascular polyp ([Gastrointest Endosc 2020;91;442](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/31499045))
* 42 year old man with a 14 cm giant fibrovascular polyp ([Acta Gastroenterol Belg 2019;82:437](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/31566334))
* 42 year old man with an 8 cm giant fibrovascular polyp ([ACG Case Rep J 2019;6:e00126](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/31620525))
* 54 year old man with a 17.5 cm giant pedunculated liposarcoma ([Int J Surg Case Rep 2019;64:113](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/31630086))
* 75 year old man with a 6.5 cm pedunculated, well differentiated liposarcoma ([Ann R Coll Surg Engl 2017;99:e209](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/28853590))

Treatment

* Endoscopic or surgical resection

Gross description

* Pedunculated polypoid (sausage shaped) mass up to 23 cm in length (average 13 cm) ([World J Gastrointest Oncol 2016;8:835](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/28035254))

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Fibroadipose tissue with slightly enlarged, hyperchromatic stromal cells, covered with intact squamous mucosa ([Mod Pathol 2018;31:337](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/28984298))
* Mostly well differentiated type / atypical lipomatous tumor ([Mod Pathol 2018;31:337](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/28984298))
* Occasionally dedifferentiated type ([Mod Pathol 2018;31:337](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/28984298))

Positive stains

* [MDM2](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsmdm2.html) and [CDK4](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainscdk4.html)

Molecular / cytogenetics description

* MDM2 and CDK4 overexpression by immunohistochemistry ([World J Gastrointest Oncol 2016;8:835](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/28035254))
* *MDM2* and *CDK4* amplification by FISH ([Mod Pathol 2018;31:337](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/28984298))
* Ring or marker chromosomes with regional amplification of chromosome 12q13-21 ([Hum Pathol 2012;43:293](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/21835434))

Sample pathology report

* Esophagus, mass, endoscopic resection:
  + Well differentiated liposarcoma / giant fibrovascular polyp (see comment)
  + Resection margin negative for liposarcoma
  + Comment: The slightly atypical stromal cells are positive for MDM2 and CDK4 by immunohistochemistry with appropriate controls. The results support the above diagnosis.

Differential diagnosis

* [Lipoma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/softtissueadiposelipoma.html):
  + Pedunculated tumor
  + Composed of mature adipose tissue with no atypical stromal cells
  + [MDM2](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsmdm2.html) and [CDK4](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainscdk4.html) testing would be negative
* [Carcinosarcoma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/esophagussarcomatoid.html):
  + Pedunculated tumor
  + Biphasic tumor with carcinomatous and sarcomatous components

Board review style question #2

Which of the following is true about giant fibrovascular polyp of the esophagus?

1. Most show *MDM2* amplification
2. They represent a reactive / nonneoplastic mass of the esophagus
3. They usually are composed of markedly pleomorphic, hyperchromatic stromal cells
4. They usually are seen in young female patients

Board review style answer #2

**A.** Most giant fibrovascular polyps show *MDM2* amplification and *CDK4* amplification supporting the diagnosis of well differentiated liposarcoma
