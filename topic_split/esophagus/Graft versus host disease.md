Definition / general

* Common after allogeneic stem cell transplantation ([Ann Hematol 2004;83:101](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/14615909))

Case reports

* Causing bullous esophagitis ([Am J Gastroenterol 1997;92:529](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/9068490))
* Acute esophageal stricture after bone marrow transplantation ([Bone Marrow Transplant 1988;3:513](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/3056556))

Gross description

* Desquamative esophagitis with web formation

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Increased intraepithelial lymphocytes, dyskeratotic squamous cells and apoptosis of individual squamous cells in noninflamed background is diagnostic
* Also have increased submucosal fibrosis associated with mucosal esophagitis and ulceration ([Gastroenterology 1981;80:914](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/7009315))
* May be disparity between severity of clinical disease and biopsy findings
* Findings may be focal, may need serial sections
