Definition / general

* Also called leukoplakic lesions, ultraviolet light related lesions
* Usually limbal conjunctiva at advancing head of pterygium

Case reports

* 42 year old man with conjunctival mass
  ([Indian J Ophthalmol 2004;52:154](http://www.ijo.in/article.asp?issn=0301-4738%3Byear=2004%3Bvolume=52%3Bissue=2%3Bspage=154%3Bepage=5%3Baulast=Jain))

  ([J Fr Ophtalmol 1992;15:65](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/1602107))
* 73 year old man with slowly progressive and whitish limbal conjunctival tumor
  ([J Fr Ophtalmol 1998;21:458](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/9759445))

Gross description

* Thickened conjunctiva with leukoplakia

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Sharply demarcated
* Mild atypia of epithelium, acanthosis, parakeratosis, hyperkeratosis but normal polarity, normal maturation
* Elastotic degeneration and lymphocytes in substantia propria
