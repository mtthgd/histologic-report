# Nodular  massive retinal gliosis

Nodular & massive retinal gliosis

Table of Contents

Definition / general

* Relatively uncommon
* Large elevated scar near disc and posterior pole after hemorrhage or inflammation
* Polyclonal - not a neoplasm

Case reports

* 32 year old woman born with bilateral microphthalmia ([Hum Pathol 2005;36:702](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/16021579))

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Vitreous body replaced by massively proliferating spindle cells containing fibrillary cytoplasm but no nuclear atypia

Positive stains

* [GFAP](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsgfap.html), [NSE](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsNSE.html) and [S100](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainss100.html) (focal)
