Definition / general

* Also called mucus membrane pemphigoid of conjunctiva
* Mucocutaneous autoimmune disorder
* Bilateral; only rarely unilateral ([Cornea 2010;29:1462](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/20847684))
* Usually age 50+ years, more common in women
* 50% of cases involve conjunctiva, usually 10 years or more after onset of cutaneous or other mucosal disease; may occasionally demonstrate purely ocular disease
  ([Ophthalmology 1999;106:355](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/9951490))
* Uncontrolled cases are associated with infection
  ([Cornea 2004;23:819](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/15502484))
* Rarely presents with corneal ulceration
  ([Eur J Ophthalmol 2007;17:121](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/17294392))

Grossing

* Impression cytology may be useful for diagnosis
  ([Arch Soc Esp Oftalmol 2004;79:67](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/14988785))
* Divide sample in two - one for formalin, one in transport media for immunofluorescent studies

Diagnosis

* Compatible clinical picture plus linear deposition of IgA and IgG along conjunctival basal lamina or circulating anti-basement membrane antibodies
  ([Ophthalmology 2004;111:45](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/14711713))
* Autoantibodies targeting α-6 and β-4 integrins
  ([Clin Dermatol 2012;30:34](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/22137224))
* Fragmented and lumpy basement membrane fibrinogen on immunofluroscence
  ([Am J Dermatopathol 2011;33:786](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/21785328))

Case reports

* 62 year old woman with anti-laminin 5 antibodies
  ([Clin Exp Dermatol 2005;30:679](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/16197388))
* 2 case reports associated with Lyell syndrome in 80 and 60 year old patients
  ([Ophthalmology 2012;119:688](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/22197436))

Microscopic (histologic) description

* **Early** - epithelial erosions and bullae
* **Late** - epithelial and stromal scarring, perivascular lymphocytes and plasma cells

Positive stains

* Local overexpression of [IL6](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/cdmarkerscd120.html#cd126), [IL12](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/cdmarkerscd210.html#cd212), [IL17](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/cdmarkerscd210.html#cdw217)
  ([Invest Ophthalmol Vis Sci. 2012;53:8112](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/23081979))

Electron microscopy description

* EM has only limited usefulness in diagnosis
  ([Ophthalmology 2006;113:1651](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/16828515))

Differential diagnosis

* Epidermolysis bullosa acquisita
  ([Arch Ophthalmol 2006;124:1615](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/17102010))
* Paraneoplastic syndrome from nonocular carcinoma
  ([Br J Ophthalmol 2007;91:1562](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/17947274))
