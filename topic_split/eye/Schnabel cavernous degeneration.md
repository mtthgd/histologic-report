Definition / general

* Defined as formation of cavernous spaces in retrolaminar portion of proximal optic nerve due to focal loss of myelin and axons and accumulation of hyaluronic acid
* Present in Associated with severe vascular anomalies (75%), glaucoma (24%)
* **Grading of cavernous degeneration:** I - clearly present but 75%
* References: [Arch Pathol Lab Med 2003;127:1314](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/14521464), [Klin Monbl Augenheilkd 1990;197:162](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/2243478)
