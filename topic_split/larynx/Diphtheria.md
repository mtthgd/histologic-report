Definition / general

* Tracheal involvement in pharyngeal / laryngeal diphtheria is uncommon
* Late appearance of grey brown thick and firm false membranes of pharynx and larynx
* Death occurs rarely in nonimmunized children ([Vestn Otorinolaringol 1995;(2):31](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/7785153))

Case reports

* Fatal case in a nonimmunized child ([Pediatr Pathol 1994;14:391](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/8065997))
