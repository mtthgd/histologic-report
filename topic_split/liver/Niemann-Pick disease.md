Definition / general

* Autosomal recessive lysosomal storage disease clinically characterized by neurodegeneration and liver disease ([Wikipedia: Niemann-Pick Disease [Accessed 29 January 2024]](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Niemann%E2%80%93Pick_disease))
* Mutations in the *NPC1* and *NPC2* genes cause impaired egress of free cholesterol from lysosomes, leading to accumulation of cholesterol and glycosphingolipids

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Foamy Kupffer cells and hepatocytes
