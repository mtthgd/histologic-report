Definition / general

* First described in 2005 ([Virchows Arch 2005;446:82](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/15480767))
* Typically women, in posterior mediastinum, showing Müllerian differentiation ([Ann Diagn Pathol 2007;11:417](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/18022126))
* Initially classified as bronchogenic or unspecified benign serous cysts

Case reports

* 45 year old woman with 5.3 cm posterior mediastinal cyst ([Case of the Week #416](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/caseofweek/case416.htm))
* 53 year old woman with previous mediastinal teratoma ([Ann Thorac Cardiovasc Surg 2012;18:39](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/21881341))

Gross description

* 1.3 - 5 cm with thin wall containing smooth muscle

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Lined by simple cylindrical or cuboidal, nonmucinous and often ciliated epithelium resembling uterine tubal epithelium
