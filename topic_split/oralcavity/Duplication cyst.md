Definition / general

* Lined by indigenous mucosa of segment it is duplicating

Case reports

* Newborn with cyst containing gastrointestinal and respiratory epithelium ([Arch Pathol Lab Med 1989;113:1301](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/2684092))
* Six infants with cysts of anterior tongue ([Arch Otolaryngol Head Neck Surg 2001;127:1484](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/11735820))
* 7 year old girl with enteric duplication cyst of floor of mouth and base of tongue ([Arch Pathol Lab Med 2000;124:614](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/10747322))

Treatment

* Excision; may recur if incompletely excised
