Definition / general

* Also called freckle
* Solitary melanotic macules, usually of lower lip
* More common in women; associated with Peutz-Jeghers syndrome

Case reports

* 4 patients with labial melanotic macule ([Dermatology 2012;224:209](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/22414589))
