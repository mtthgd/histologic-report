Definition / general

* Also called Heck disease
* Discrete, recurrent papillary lesions on oral mucosa in Native American and Inuit children, associated with HPV 1, 13, 32 ([Eur J Dermatol 2011;21:396](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/21697033))

Case reports

* 7 year old girl with HPV 32 related disease ([J Maxillofac Oral Surg 2011;10:357](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/23204755))

Gross description

* Well circumscribed, sessile and pale elevation of buccal mucosa

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Balloon cells in Malpighian layers, localized areas of mucosal epithelial hyperplasia with marked acanthosis and parakeratosis
