Definition / general

* Resembles carcinoma clinically
* Presumed due to crush injury of tongue muscle

Terminology

* Also called ulcerative eosinophilic granuloma, Riga-Fede disease, traumatic granuloma

Case reports

* 6 month old boy with traumatic lingual ulceration ([Ital J Pediatr 2012;38:20](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/22621646))
* 70 year old woman with Riga-Fede-like disease ([Indian J Dermatol 2010;55:92](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/20418986))

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Eosinophils and other inflammatory cells in submucosa, mucosa, salivary glands
* Dilated vessels often present
