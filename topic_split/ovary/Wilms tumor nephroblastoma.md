Definition / general

* See also topics in [Kidney tumor](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/kidneytumor.html) chapter: [Childhood Wilms](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/kidneytumorwilmkids.html), [Adult Wilms](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/kidneytumorwilmkids.html)
* Extrarenal Wilms tumor are very rare, mostly seen in children
* Occur mostly in reteroperitoneum and very rarely in ovary
* Hypothesis for origin of these tumors is still debated

Epidemiology

* To date, limited case reports:
  + 14 months ([Journal of Gynecologic Surgery 2012;28:306](http://online.liebertpub.com/doi/abs/10.1089/gyn.2011.0093))
  + 3.5 years ([J Pediatr Surg 2002;37:127](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/11782005))
  + 21 years ([Hum Pathol 2000;31:761](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/10872672))
  + 56 years ([Cancer 1988;61:1460](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/2830959))

Sites

* Usually retroperitoneal and inguinal regions ([J Pediatr Surg 2002;37:127](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/11782005))

Pathophysiology

* Hypotheses regarding the origin: see [J Indian Assoc Pediatr Surg 2007;12:145](http://www.jiaps.com/article.asp?issn=0971-9261;year=2007;volume=12;issue=3;spage=145;epage=147;aulast=Ramachandra;aid=JIndianAssocPediatrSurg_2007_12_3_145_34956)
  + From ectopic metanephric blastema
  + From primitive mesodermal tissue
  + Connheim's cell rest theory

Clinical features

* Palpable mass

Diagnosis

* The diagnostic criteria necessary to establish the diagnosis include absence of primary kidney tumor and supernumerary kidney (radiologically and surgically)

Radiology description

* USG and CT: solid mass with cystic components ([J Pediatr Surg 2002;37:127](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/11782005))

Prognostic factors

* When matched with the appropriate stage, prognosis is similar to its renal counterpart ([J Indian Assoc Pediatr Surg 2007;12:145](http://www.jiaps.com/article.asp?issn=0971-9261;year=2007;volume=12;issue=3;spage=145;epage=147;aulast=Ramachandra;aid=JIndianAssocPediatrSurg_2007_12_3_145_34956))

Treatment

* Excision and chemotherapy
* Similar staging and treatment protocol to renal Wilms tumor ([J Clin Oncol 1991;9:167](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/1845874))

Gross description

* Solid large mass with cystic cavities
