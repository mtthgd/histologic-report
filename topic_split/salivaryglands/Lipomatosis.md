Definition / general

* Diffuse deposition of adipose tissue (usually throughout parotid gland) with overall enlargement of gland but no distinct mass
* Associated with alcoholism, cirrhosis, diabetes, hormonal abnormalities, malnutrition
* May be preceded by sialadenosis (acinar cell hypertrophy, interstitial edema, ductal atrophy)
* Slow growing, usually in parotid gland, may affect children
* May recur

Differential diagnosis

* [Myoepithelioma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/salivaryglandsmyoepithelioma.html): some cases have coexisting lipomatosis
* [Pleomorphic adenoma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/salivaryglandspleomorphicadenoma.html): some cases have lipometaplasia or lipomatous foci ([Am J Surg Pathol 2005;29:1389](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/16160483))
