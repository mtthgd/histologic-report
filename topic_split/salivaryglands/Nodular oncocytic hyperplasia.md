Definition / general

* Either parotid cysts lined by oncocytes or well defined clusters of oncocytes (mm to cm)
* First reported by Schwartz ([Cancer 1969;23:636](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/5766505))
* Also called multinodular oncocytoma, multifocal adenomatous oncocytic hyperplasia

Clinical features

* Benign, multifocal / nodular or diffuse proliferation of oncocytic cells, usually in parotid gland
* May be bilateral

Case reports

* 68 year old man with 6 cm parotid swelling ([J Cytol 2012;29:80](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/22438628))
* 78 year old man ([Laryngorhinootologie 2004;83:185](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/15042484))

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Either parotid cysts lined by oncocytes or well defined clusters of oncocytes (mm to cm)
* Oncocytes (oxyphilic cells) are large ductal epithelial cells with eosinophilic granular cytoplasm

Differential diagnosis

* **Normal aging**: increased oncocytes
* [Oncocytoma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/salivaryglandsoncocytoma.html): distinct mass
* [Rhabdomyoma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/softtissueadultrhabdomyoma.html): may resemble at intraoperative frozen section; positive for skeletal muscle markers; different morphology on permanent section ([Arch Pathol Lab Med 1983;107:638](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/6357142))
* [Sialadenosis](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/salivaryglandsadenomatoidhyperplasia.html): may resemble diffuse oncocytosis ([Laryngol Rhinol Otol (Stuttg) 1982;61:691](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/7154807))
* [Warthin tumor](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/salivaryglandswarthin.html): double layer of epithelial cells resting on dense lymphoid stroma with variable germinal centers
