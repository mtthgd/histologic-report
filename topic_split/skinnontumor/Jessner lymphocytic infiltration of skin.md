Definition / general

* May be a variant of lymphoid hyperplasia
* Related to discoid lupus erythematosus
* Violaceous nodules of head and neck or trunk

Case reports

* 78 year old man with 1 year history of bilateral painless runny eyes ([BMJ Case Rep 2010;2010:bcr07.2009.2064](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/22347890))
