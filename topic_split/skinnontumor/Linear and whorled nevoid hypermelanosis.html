Definition / general
  • Rare skin disorder, often in first weeks of life, with swirls and streaks of macular hyperpigmentation along lines of Blaschko (Wikipedia)
  • Usually not associated with other abnormalities (Pediatr Dermatol 2007;24:205)

Clinical features
  • Swirls and whorls of hyperpigmented macules in reticulate pattern along Blaschko lines
Case reports
  • 3 month old boy with diffuse hyperpigmentation (J Pediatr 2012;160:171)
  • 7 year old boy with trisomy 20 mosaicism (Pediatr Dermatol 2004;21:636)
  • 20 year old man with widespread involvement (Dermatol Online J 2007;13:23)
  • Familial case of 33 year old woman and her 3 month old daughter (J Am Acad Dermatol 1994;30:831)
Treatment
  • No satisfactory treatment
