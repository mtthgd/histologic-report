Definition / general

* Rare skin disorder, often in first weeks of life, with swirls and streaks of macular hyperpigmentation along lines of Blaschko ([Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Blaschko%27s_lines))
* Usually not associated with other abnormalities ([Pediatr Dermatol 2007;24:205](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/17542865))

Clinical features

* Swirls and whorls of hyperpigmented macules in reticulate pattern along Blaschko lines

Case reports

* 3 month old boy with diffuse hyperpigmentation ([J Pediatr 2012;160:171](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/21868034))
* 7 year old boy with trisomy 20 mosaicism ([Pediatr Dermatol 2004;21:636](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/15575846))
* 20 year old man with widespread involvement ([Dermatol Online J 2007;13:23](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/18328217))
* Familial case of 33 year old woman and her 3 month old daughter ([J Am Acad Dermatol 1994;30:831](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/8169255))

Treatment

* No satisfactory treatment
