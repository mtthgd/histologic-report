Definition / general
  • Most frequent type of porphyria; autosomal dominant disorder due to catalytic deficiency of uroporphyrinogen decarboxylase (UROD), the fifth enzyme in heme biosynthesis; reduced complement deposition during clinical remission (Best Pract Res Clin Gastroenterol 2010;24:735, An Bras Dermatol 2010;85:827)

Microscopic (histologic) description
  • Dermal papillae protrudes into bulla with festooned pattern; roof of blister has eosinophilic, PAS+ and diastase resistant linear globules
