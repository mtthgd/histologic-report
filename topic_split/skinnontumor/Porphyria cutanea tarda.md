Definition / general

* Most frequent type of porphyria; autosomal dominant disorder due to catalytic deficiency of uroporphyrinogen decarboxylase (UROD), the fifth enzyme in heme biosynthesis; reduced complement deposition during clinical remission ([Best Pract Res Clin Gastroenterol 2010;24:735](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/20955974), [An Bras Dermatol 2010;85:827](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/21308306))

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Dermal papillae protrudes into bulla with festooned pattern; roof of blister has eosinophilic, PAS+ and diastase resistant linear globules
