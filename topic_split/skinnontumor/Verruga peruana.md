Definition / general

* Verruga peruana and Oroya fever are now called Carrión disease ([Wikipedia](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Carrion%27s_disease))
* Due to infection by Bartonella bacilliformis, a gram negative, flagellated, motile bacteria ([J Infect Dis 1992;165:1138](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/1583335), [Future Microbiol 2009;4:743](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/19659429))
* Endemic in Peru
