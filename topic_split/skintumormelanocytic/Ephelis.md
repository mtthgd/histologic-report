Definition / general

* Benign lesion of sun exposed skin, associated with fair skin and red hair ([Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Freckle))
* Appears in early childhood; fades in winter, reappears in summer
* Due to variations in melanocortin 1 receptor gene ([Hum Mol Genet 2001;10:1701](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/11487574))
* Risk factor for skin cancer ([Int J Cancer 2010;127:2430](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/20143394))

Terminology

* Plural is ephelides

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Mild hyperpigmentation of basal keratinocytes, normal architecture
* No increase in number of melanocytes
