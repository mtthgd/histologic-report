Definition / general

* Rare variant characterized by morphologic features of nevus ([Hum Pathol 1995;26:171](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/7860047), [Arch Dermatol Res 1985;277:362](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/4026378), [Mod Pathol 2006;19:S41](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/16446716))

Terminology

* Synonym: "verrucous pseudonevoid melanoma"
* Distinct from controversial term "minimal deviation melanoma"

Epidemiology

* Adults in fifth decade
* No clear gender preponderance ([Clin Dermatol 2009;27:564](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/19880044), [Mod Pathol 2006;19:S41](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/16446716))

Clinical features

* Rare variant characterized by morphologic features of nevus
* Behavior similar to other melanomas; may recur or metastasize causing death ([Am J Dermatopathol 2001;23:167](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/11391094))
* Key to diagnosis is high index of suspicion
* Dome shaped papule, nodule or verrucous lesion
* Tan nodule 1 cm or larger on trunk or proximal limbs of young adult

Case reports

* 4 year old girl with metastatic tumor to sentinel lymph node ([J Cutan Pathol 2003;30:647](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/14744091))
* Multiple primary nevoid melanomas in HIV / AIDS patient ([J Am Acad Dermatol 2002;47:S172](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/12140453))

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Resembles ordinary compound or dermal nevus at low power, with symmetrical dome shaped or verrucous and papillomatous features, sharp lateral demarcation, inconspicuous junctional component and no pagetoid growth
* High power shows relatively bland and monomorphic cells resembling classic nevus or epithelioid cells in Spitz nevus
* Focal sheetlike growth pattern, nucleoli in tumor cells at base of lesion
* Multiple dermal mitoses with atypical mitoses
* Subtle pleomorphism and impaired maturation with depth

Positive stains

* [HMB45](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainshmb45.html) (strong throughout) ([J Cutan Pathol 1995;22:502](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/8835170))
* Increased labeling index with [Ki67](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainski67.html)
* Diffuse staining with [cyclin D1](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainscyclind1.html)

Differential diagnosis

* **Melanoma arising in dermal nevus**:
  + Residual nevus present, often tumor extension into deep reticular dermis and fat
* **Metastatic melanoma**
* **Minimal deviation melanoma**:
  + At most moderate atypia, at least level III due to dermal invasion, remnants of existing nevi usually present and usually few mitotic figures
* [Nodular melanoma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/skintumormelanocyticnodularmelanoma.html):
  + High grade atypia, intraepidermal atypia
