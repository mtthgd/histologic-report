Definition / general

* Superficial dermal based smooth muscle tumor which is biologically different from deep [leiomyosarcoma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/softtissueleiomyosarcoma.html)
* Typically larger than leiomyomas with vascular invasion
* Recurs, but only rarely metastasizes
* May be associated with HIV infection

Case reports

* 59 year old woman with painless skin nodule ([J Med Case Reports 2007;1:180](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/18081931))
* 67 year old woman with neck lesion ([Cases J 2010;3:52](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/20205848))

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Cellular lesions of smooth muscle type cells with atypia, necrosis and mitotic activity
* May have prominent vascular pattern, clear cell features, desmoplasia
