Definition / general

* Polypoid or dome-shaped cutaneous nodule with sparse cellularity and cytologic atypia of fibroblasts
* Not a WHO diagnosis
* First described in 1989 ([Am J Surg Pathol 1989;13:107](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/2916726))
* Usually trunk, extremity or head ([Clin Exp Dermatol 1998;23:22](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/9667104))
* **Related entities:** may be a variant of skin tag (acrochordon) with cytologic atypia

Clinical features

* Flesh-colored polyp resembling skin tag (acrochordon)

Case reports

* 38 year old woman with a flesh-colored forehead nodule ([Arch Pathol Lab Med 2005;129:e21](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/15628922))
* 38 year old man ([J Cytol 2013;30:71](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/23661949))
* 66 year old woman with subungual tumor ([J Cutan Pathol 2003;30:569](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/14507406))
* 75 year old man with myxoid pleomorphic fibroma that supposedly progressed to myxofibrosarcoma ([Australas J Dermatol 2003;44:287](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/14616499))

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Resembles fibroepithelial polyp but with enlarged, bizarre, smudged, hyperchromatic nuclei, thick collagen bundles and rare mitotic figures; may be sclerotic ([Am J Dermatopathol 2002;24:54](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/11803283)) or have myxoid foci ([Am J Dermatopathol 1998;20:502](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/9790115))

Differential diagnosis

* [Angiofibroma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/softtissueangiofibroma.html): pleomorphic cells are similar, but have marked vascularity
* [Atypical fibrous histiocytoma / dermatofibroma with monster cells](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/softtissuebfh.html): storiform pattern, more cellular, foam cells, hemosiderin laden macrophages, CD34- ([Am J Dermatopathol 1999;21:414](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/10535568))
* [Atypical fibroxanthoma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/skintumornonmelanocyticAFX.html): more cellular, more mitotic figures
* [Atypical lipomatous tumor](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/softtissuewdliposarcoma.html): may be in differential when pleomorphic fibroma has entrapped adipose cells, but atypical lipomatous tumor has 12q15/MDM2 amplification by FISH ([J Cutan Pathol 2013;40:379](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/23506010))
* [Dermal pleomorphic liposarcoma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/softtissueadiposepleomorphiclipo.html): high-grade sarcoma with pleomorphic lipoblasts ([J Cutan Pathol 2013;40:734](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/23651098), [Am J Surg Pathol 2012;36:1047](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/22472959))
* [Giant cell fibroblastoma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/softtissuegiantcellfibro.html): young children
