Lymphocytic ileitis: ileal biopsy from 28 year old man with chronic diarrhea

Lymphocytic colitis related

* High intraepithelial lymphocyte count in terminal ileum biopsies ([Mod Pathol 2003;16:115](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/12591963), [Am J Surg Pathol 2002;26:1484](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/12409725))
* Normal range of ileal lymphocytes is 0 - 9 lymphocytes/100 enterocytes, decreases with age ([Am J Clin Pathol 2007;127:816](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/17439842))
* Lymphocytes are mostly suppressor T cells
* **Symptoms:** chronic nonbloody diarrhea, relatively normal endoscopy
