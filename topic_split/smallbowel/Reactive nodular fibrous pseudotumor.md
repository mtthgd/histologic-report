Definition / general

* First described in 2003 in 5 patients (4 men), mean age 56 years ([Am J Surg Pathol 2003;27:532](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/12657940))
* May present as obstruction ([Am Surg 2011;77:790](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/21679652)) but otherwise benign clinical course

Gross description

* Firm, tan white, 4 - 6 cm, solitary / multiple, in small bowel or colon

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Low / moderate cellularity
* Spindled fibroblasts arranged haphazardly or in intersecting fascicles
* Often infiltrative borders
* Stroma rich in wire-like, keloidal or hyalinized collagen
* Peripheral lymphoid aggregates

Positive stains

* [Vimentin](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsvimentin.html), [CD117](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/cdmarkerscd117.html) or [muscle specific actin](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsactinmusclespecific.html) (80%), [smooth muscle actin](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsalphasmoothmuscleactin.html) or [desmin](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsdesmin.html) (60%) ([Int J Surg Pathol 2004;12:365](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/15494862))

Negative stains

* [CD34](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/cdmarkerscd34.html), [S100](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainss100.html), [ALK1](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsalk.html)

Differential diagnosis

* [Fibromatosis](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/colontumorfibromatosis.html)
