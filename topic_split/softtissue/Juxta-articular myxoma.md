Definition / general

* Myxoid lesion arising adjacent to large joint ([Hum Pathol 1992;23:639](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/1592386), [Stanford University: Juxta-articular Myxoma](http://surgpathcriteria.stanford.edu/softmisc/juxta-articular_myxoma/printable.html))
* Often associated with cystic changes resembling ganglion cyst
* Often recurs up to 4 times

Epidemiology

* Median age 43 years, range 16 - 83 years
* 72% male

Sites

* Knee (88%), also shoulder, elbow, ankle, hip

Case reports

* 5 year old boy with palpable mass on medial side of knee proximal to knee joint ([Eur Radiol 2010;20:764](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/20157818))
* 63 year old man runner with suprapatellar symptoms ([Orthopedics 2009;32:527](https://www.healio.com/orthopedics/journals/ortho/2009-7-32-7/%7Bb60e75cb-b7c7-40dd-9e09-4a4a003cd7e1%7D/juxta-articular-myxoma-within-the-suprapatellar-pouch-masquerading-as-a-ganglion-cyst))
* 68 year old athletic woman with several month history of progressing pain, swelling, and loss of motion in knee ([Arthroscopy 2003;19:E6](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/14673463))
