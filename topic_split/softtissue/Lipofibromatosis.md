Definition / general

* Pediatric tumor with predilection for hands and feet, composed predominantly of mature adipose tissue with proliferative fibroblasts in adipose septa ([Stanford University](http://surgpathcriteria.stanford.edu/softfat/lipofibromatosis/printable.html))
* Previously called infantile fibromatosis of non-desmoid type
* First described in 2000 using "lipofibromatosis" ([Am J Surg Pathol 2000;24:1491](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/11075850))

Epidemiology

* Rare childhood tumor (first surgery usually at age 1), 2/3 male, often of distal extremities

Clinical features

* Associated with macrodactyly of foot ([Foot Ankle 1991;12:40](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/1959834))
* Clinically resembles lymphatic malformation or lymphedema
* Recurs locally due to lack of securing clean margins at resection, no metastases

Case reports

* Infant boy with lipofibromatosis in foot ([Skeletal Radiol 2008;37:555](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/18278490))
* Lipofibromatosis arising in a pediatric forearm ([Ups J Med Sci 2005;110:259](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/16454165))

Gross description

* White-tan or yellow with obvious fatty component, 1 - 3 cm

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Lobules of mature adipose tissue with fibroblastic foci consisting of bland fibroblasts involving adipose septa with a preserved lobular architecture
* Often have minute small univacuolated cells at interface between fibroblasts and adipose
* No atypia, no/rare mitotic figures
* Rarely pigmented cells are associated with fibroblastic element; resemble those in Bednar tumor, pigmented neurofibroma, nevi

Positive stains

* **Spindle cells:** [CD34](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/cdmarkerscd34.html), [CD99](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/cdmarkerscd99.html), [smooth muscle actin](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsalphasmoothmuscleactin.html)
* Variable [BCL2](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsbcl2.html), [S100](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainss100.html), [EMA](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsema.html), [muscle specific actin](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsactinmusclespecific.html)

Negative stains

* [Desmin](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsdesmin.html), [keratin](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsckgeneral.html), [beta-catenin](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsbetacatenin.html)

Molecular / cytogenetics description

* Rare t(4;9;6) translocation in 5 year old boy ([Cancer Genet Cytogenet 2007;179:136](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/18036401))

Differential diagnosis

* [Fibromatosis](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/softtissuefibromatosisdeep.html): solid sheet-like fibrous growth, minimal to no fat
* [Fibrous hamartoma of infancy](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/softtissuefibroushamart.html): has primitive oval cell component with myxoid stroma
