Definition / general
  • Arteriovenous malformation is rare
  • Dieulafoy lesion
    • Usually single, submucosal, high on lesser curvature (Am J Surg Pathol 1982;6:83)
    • Complications: perforation, hemorrhage
Terminology
  • Dieulafoy lesion is also called caliber persistent artery, Dieulafoy disease
Case reports
  • 74 year old man with amyloid deposition in a gastric arteriovenous malformation (Arch Pathol Lab Med 1986;110:69)
Microscopic (histologic) description
  • Dieulafoy lesion
    • Large tortuous vessel with small defect in overlying mucosa
    • May have amyloid (Ann Pathol 2004;24:256)
