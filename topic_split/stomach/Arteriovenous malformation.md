Definition / general

* Arteriovenous malformation is rare
* Dieulafoy lesion
  + Usually single, submucosal, high on lesser curvature ([Am J Surg Pathol 1982;6:83](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/6979262))
  + Complications: perforation, hemorrhage

Terminology

* Dieulafoy lesion is also called caliber persistent artery, Dieulafoy disease

Case reports

* 74 year old man with amyloid deposition in a gastric arteriovenous malformation ([Arch Pathol Lab Med 1986;110:69](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/3753573))

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Dieulafoy lesion
  + Large tortuous vessel with small defect in overlying mucosa
  + May have amyloid ([Ann Pathol 2004;24:256](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/15480260))
