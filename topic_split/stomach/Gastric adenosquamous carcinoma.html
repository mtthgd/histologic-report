Gastric adenosquamous carcinoma
Table of Contents
Definition / general | Prognostic factors | Microscopic (histologic) description | Positive stains
Definition / general
  • Rare; neoplastic squamous component at least 25% of tumor
  • Behavior determined by glandular component
Prognostic factors
  • Generally poor prognosis
Microscopic (histologic) description
  • By definition, neoplastic squamous component at least 25% of tumor; generally transitional zone between squamous and glandular component; lymphovascular invasion common
Positive stains
  • CD44 (squamous component) (Arch Pathol Lab Med 2000;124:212)
