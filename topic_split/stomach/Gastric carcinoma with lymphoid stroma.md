Definition / general

* Gastric carcinoma composed of small nests of cancer cells broadly distributed in a background of dense and prominent lymphoid stroma, histologicically similar to nasopharyngeal carcinoma ([Arch Pathol Lab Med 2008;132:706](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/18384225))

Essential features

* Infrequent subtype of gastric cancer composed of small trabeculae and nests of epithelial cells embedded in a dense lymphoid infiltrate reminiscent of lymphoid tissue
* Strongly associated with Epstein-Barr virus (EBV) infection and microsatellite instability (mutually exclusive)
* PDL1 is usually overexpressed, making these tumors prone to immune checkpoint blockade therapy

Terminology

* Lymphoepithelioma-like carcinoma
* Medullary carcinoma
* Undifferentiated carcinoma with lymphoid stroma

ICD coding

* ICD-O: [8140/3](https://apps.who.int/iris/bitstream/handle/10665/96612/9789241548496_eng.pdf?sequence=1#page=65) - adenocarcinoma, NOS

Epidemiology

* Constitutes about 4% of all gastric carcinomas ([Arch Pathol Lab Med 2008;132:706](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/18384225))
* Mean age 70 years ([Am J Surg Pathol 2018;42:453](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/29438172), [J Surg Res 2017;210:159](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/28457323))
* Strongly associated with Epstein-Barr virus (EBV) infection and microsatellite instability (MSI) ([Am J Surg Pathol 2018;42:453](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/29438172))
* EBV positive cases are more frequent in men ([Gastric Cancer 2015;18:246](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/24771002))
* MSI associated cases are more frequent in women and older patients ([ESMO Open 2019;4:e000470](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/31231566))

Sites

* EBV associated cases are frequently located in the cardia and middle portion of the stomach ([Arch Pathol Lab Med 2008;132:706](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/18384225))
* Microsatellite instability associated cases are more common in the antrum ([Arch Pathol Lab Med 2008;132:706](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/18384225), [ESMO Open 2019;4:e000470](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/31231566))

Pathophysiology

* EBV seems to be involved in the early stage of carcinogenesis and remains expressed in all primary tumor cells and their metastases ([Arch Pathol Lab Med 2008;132:706](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/18384225))
* EBV infection of gastric cells by lymphocytes with reactivated EBV is suspected to be the first step of EBV associated gastric carcinoma development ([Cancer Sci 2008;99:195](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/18271915))
* EBV expression consistently absent in normal epithelium or dysplastic lesions ([J Exp Clin Cancer Res 2009;28:14](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/19192297), [Arch Pathol Lab Med 2008;132:706](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/18384225))

Etiology

* EBV and microsatellite instability in gastric carcinoma are mutually exclusive ([Am J Surg Pathol 2018;42:453](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/29438172))
* In Asia, most cases (around 80%) are related to EBV infection, a significantly higher rate compared with Western countries (7 - 39%) ([Gastric Cancer 2015;18:246](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/24771002), [Am J Surg Pathol 2018;42:453](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/29438172), [ESMO Open 2019;4:e000470](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/31231566))
  + EBV can be demonstrated in 1.8 - 18% of all sporadic gastric carcinomas ([Mod Pathol 2003;16:641](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/12861059), [ESMO Open 2019;4:e000470](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/31231566))
* Around 38% of cases are associated with microsatellite instability ([Am J Surg Pathol 2018;42:453](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/29438172))
  + Microsatellite instability is detected in 15 - 30% of all gastric carcinomas ([J Surg Oncol 2018;117:829](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/29534305), [ESMO Open 2019;4:e000470](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/31231566))

Diagnosis

* Although most cases seems to be localized at the time of diagnosis, distinctive clinical signs and symptoms associated with this type of tumor have not been reported ([Am J Surg Pathol 2018;42:453](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/29438172), [J Surg Res 2017;210:159](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/28457323))
* Endoscopy: frequently described as a hypoechoic lesion in the submucosa and the muscularis mucosa, mimicking features of subepithelial tumors ([World J Gastroenterol 2014;20:1365](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/24574813))

Radiology description

* CT scan: well circumscribed mass with a large thickness to length ratio with the low density stripe of the normal gastric wall abruptly terminated at the edge of the lesion ([Medicine (Baltimore) 2019;98:e14839](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/30946311))

Prognostic factors

* Microsatellite instability associated cases have a better outcome (reduced risk of mortality) independently of other factors such as clinical stage or nodal metastasis ([ESMO Open 2019;4:e000470](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/31231566), [Appl Immunohistochem Mol Morphol 2017;25:12](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/26371427))
* EBV positive cases have a lower incidence of lymph node metastasis and seem to show longer overall survival, although other results are contradictory ([J Surg Res 2017;210:159](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/28457323), [Gastric Cancer 2015;18:246](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/24771002), [J Surg Oncol 2018;117:829](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/29534305), [ESMO Open 2019;4:e000470](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/31231566))

Case reports

* 41 year old man with a submucosal mass in the gastric antrum ([Clin Gastroenterol Hepatol 2018;16:e87](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/29574027))
* 53 year old man with epithelioid granulomas in a gastric carcinoma with lymphoid stroma ([Oncol Lett 2013;5:549](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/23420641))
* 56 year old HIV positive woman with a gastric mass ([Pathology 2009;41:593](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/19900113))
* 64 year old man with a gastric mass and previous history of EBV associated nasopharyngeal undifferentiated carcinoma ([Pathology 2010;42:684](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/21080882))

Treatment

* Localized cases are prone to curative submucosal dissection ([World J Gastroenterol 2014;20:1365](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/24574813))
* High sensitivity to immune checkpoint blockade therapy with pembrolizumab (anti-PD1 antibody) has been observed in advanced EBV and microsatellite instability gastric cancer (overall response rate of 85.7% in microsatellite instability and overall response rate of 100% in EBV positive cases) ([Nat Med 2018;24:1449](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/30013197))

Gross description

* Large ulcerated tumors with well delineated margins and pushing borders ([Arch Pathol Lab Med 2008;132:706](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/18384225), [J Surg Res 2017;210:159](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/28457323))

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Dense lymphoid infiltrate in a nondesmoplastic stroma reminiscent of lymphoid tissue (mean 500 tumor infiltrating lymphocytes per high power field) ([Mod Pathol 2003;16:641](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/12861059))
* Tumor cells are large and oval, contain vesicular to clear nuclei, have prominent nucleoli and abundant eosinophilic cytoplasm with poorly defined cell borders ([Arch Pathol Lab Med 2008;132:706](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/18384225))
* Neoplastic cells are arranged primarily in microalveolar, thin trabecular and primitive tubular patterns uniformly distributed throughout the lymphoid stroma ([Arch Pathol Lab Med 2008;132:706](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/18384225))
* Discrete areas of glandular differentiation may be seen ([Am J Surg Pathol 2018;42:453](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/29438172))
* Small lymphocytes can also infiltrate into cancer cell nests ([Arch Pathol Lab Med 2008;132:706](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/18384225))
* Epithelioid granulomas are sometimes observed within the lymphoid stroma ([Arch Pathol Lab Med 2008;132:706](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/18384225))
* May rarely show osteoclast-like giant cells ([Pathol Int 2010;60:551](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/20618732))

Positive stains

* [CD3](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/cdmarkerscd3.html) and [CD8](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/cdmarkerscd8.html): the lymphocytes infiltrating into tumor cell nests are predominantly cytotoxic ([Cancer Lett 2003;200:33](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/14550950))
* Microsatellite instability can be evaluated immunohistochemically with the use of commercially available antibodies for the mismatch repair proteins ([MSH2](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsmsh2.html), [MSH6](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsmsh6.html), [MLH1](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsmlh1.html) and [PMS2](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainspms2.html)) ([ESMO Open 2019;4:e000470](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/31231566))
* EBV encoded RNA (EBER) in situ hybridization is the most sensitive method for detecting EBV in paraffin embedded tissues and stains all tumor cells ([Arch Pathol Lab Med 2008;132:706](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/18384225), [Cancer Lett 2003;200:33](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/14550950))
* [PDL1](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsPDL1.html) / **L2** and their receptors ([PD1](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainspd1.html) / **2**) are frequently overexpressed in [EBV](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainseber1.html) gastric cancer ([Oncotarget 2016;7:32925](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/27147580))

Negative stains

* [HER2](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsbreasther2.html) is usually negative ([J Surg Res 2017;210:159](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/28457323), [Am J Surg Pathol 2018;42:453](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/29438172))
* [LMP1](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainseber1.html#lmp) is negative in most cases ([J Exp Clin Cancer Res 2009;28:14](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/19192297))

Molecular / cytogenetics description

* For microsatellite instability evaluation in gastric cancer, the immunohistochemical study of MMR proteins and PCR show an excellent concordance ([ESMO Open 2019;4:e000470](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/31231566))
* EBV positive tumors show the higher prevalence of DNA hypermethylation among all gastric tumors ([Nature 2014;513:202](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/25079317))
* The main molecular alterations in EBV associated cases include hypermethylation of the promoter region of the gene *CDKN2A* (p16INK4A) and mutation in phosphatidylinositol-3-kinase catalytic subunit alpha (*PIK3CA*) in about 5 - 10% ([Oncotarget 2016;7:32925](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/27147580), [Nature 2014;513:202](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/25079317), [ESMO Open 2019;4:e000470](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/31231566))
* *MLH1* hypermethylation is characteristic of microsatellite instability associated cases ([Nature 2014;513:202](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/25079317))
* Microsatellite instability cases show high tumor mutation burden and *KRAS* alterations (56%) ([Am J Surg Pathol 2018;42:453](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/29438172))

Sample pathology report

* Gastric mass, upper body, endoscopic biopsy:
  + Gastric carcinoma with lymphoid stroma
  + In situ hybridization study for EBER is diffusely positive in all tumor cells

Differential diagnosis

* [Reactive lymphoid hyperplasia](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/lymphnodesfollicularhyperplasia.html):
  + No epithelial component ([AE1 / AE3](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsae1ae3.html) immunostaining can confirm)
  + Lymphoid infiltrate in gastric carcinoma with lymphoid stroma may obscure the neoplastic proliferation ([Arch Pathol Lab Med 2008;132:706](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/18384225))
* [Mucosal associated marginal zone lymphoma (MALT lymphoma)](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stomachMALT.html):
  + Intraepithelial lymphocytes may mimic the appearance of lymphoepithelial lesions, especially in superficial and small endoscopic biopsies
  + Distinction is based on the recognition of atypia in epithelial cells ([Arch Pathol Lab Med 2008;132:706](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/18384225))
  + [AE1 / AE3](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsae1ae3.html) is useful to evaluate the broad distribution of malignant epithelial cells
  + Predominant [CD3](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/cdmarkerscd3.html)+ T cell lymphoid population helps to exclude a MALT lymphoma, which is a B cell proliferation
