Definition / general

* Rare, biphasic tumor of the stomach (only 13 cases currently reported) ([Genes Chromosomes Cancer 2021;60:640](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/34041825))

Essential features

* Rare, biphasic epitheliomesenchymal neoplasm of the stomach
* Majority of cases have characteristic fusion gene, *MALAT1::GLI1*
* Novel *EWSR1::CTBP1* fusion was recently identified
* Mostly seen in young adults

ICD coding

* ICD-O: [8976/3](https://seer.cancer.gov/icd-o-3/errata.sitetype.20210607.pdf?sequence=1#page=3) - gastroblastoma
* ICD-11: [2F70.1 & XH4VQ1](https://icd.who.int/browse11/l-m/en#/http%3a%2f%2fid.who.int%2ficd%2fentity%2f1405726020?data=%7B%22dataType%22%3A%22pc%22%2C%22postcoordinationCodeSet%22%3A%7B%22stemId%22%3A%22http%3A%2F%2Fid.who.int%2Ficd%2Fentity%2F1405726020%22%2C%22axisToValueIds%22%3A%7B%22specificAnatomy%22%3A%5B%7B%22stemId%22%3A%22http%3A%2F%2Fid.who.int%2Ficd%2Fentity%2F818735541%22%7D%5D%7D%7D%7D) - neoplasms of uncertain behavior of stomach & gastroblastoma

Epidemiology

* Young adults, most F ([Histopathology 2019;75:778](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/31287169), [Mod Pathol 2017;30:1443](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/28731043))

Sites

* Stomach (intramural), antrum > body ([Histopathology 2019;75:778](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/31287169))

Pathophysiology

* *MALAT1::GLI1* fusion drives the oncogenic properties of *GLI1* and activates hedgehog pathway ([Genes Chromosomes Cancer 2022;61:285](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/35239225))
* *EWSR1::CTBP1* fusion drives the NOTCH and FGFR pathway activation ([Genes Chromosomes Cancer 2022;61:285](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/35239225))

Etiology

* Unknown

Clinical features

* Nonspecific symptoms at presentation ([Am J Surg Pathol 2009;33:1370](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/19718790))

Diagnosis

* Imaging followed by tissue diagnosis

Radiology description

* CT: solid and cystic heterogenous mass centered in gastric wall or exophytic ([Radiographics 2014;34:1929](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/25384293))

Case reports

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Biphasic tumor
  + Spindle cell component demonstrating sheets of monotonous spindled type cells
  + Epithelial component demonstrating cords / clusters of epithelial type cells, some with vague to well formed gland formation, which may contain inspissated material
* Relatively low mitotic rate ([Am J Surg Pathol 2009;33:1370](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/19718790))

Sample pathology report

* Stomach, partial gastrectomy:
  + Gastroblastoma (see comment)
  + Comment: H&E sections demonstrate a biphasic tumor with varied intermixed epithelial and stromal elements. The epithelial component is positive for keratin AE1 / AE3 while the stromal component is positive for vimentin. Both components are negative for KIT, CD34, desmin, S100, CD99 and SMA. Additional ancillary testing demonstrates the *MALAT1::GLI1* fusion gene.

Differential diagnosis

* [Biphasic synovial sarcoma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/softtissuesynovialsarc.html):
  + Usually monophasic in stomach ([Am J Surg Pathol 2009;33:1370](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/19718790))
  + Translocation t(x;18), *SYT::SSX1*
* [Carcinosarcoma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/esophagussarcomatoid.html):
  + Overtly malignant epithelial and stromal tumor
  + No characteristic translocation
* [Gastrointestinal stromal tumor (GIST)](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stomachGIST.html):
  + [CD117](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/cdmarkerscd117.html) and [DOG1](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsDOG1.html) positive
* [Plexiform fibromyxoma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stomachplexiformfibromyxoma.html):
  + Monophasic
  + Plexiform growth pattern
  + Can exhibit *MALAT1::GLI1* fusion

Board review style question #1

Which fusion gene typifies gastroblastoma?

1. *ASPL::TFE3*
2. *BRAF::KIAA1549*
3. *ETV6(TEL)::NTRK3*
4. *MALAT1::GLI1*
5. *SYT::SSX1*

Board review style answer #1

**D.** *MALAT1::GLI1*
