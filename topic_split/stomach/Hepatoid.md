Hepatoid

Definition / general

* Subtype with hepatocellular differentiation and poor prognosis

Gross description

* Nodular or massive growth, extensive venous invasion

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Glandular and hepatocellular differentiation
* Abundant cytoplasmic glycogen, hyaline globules
* May have tubulopapillary pattern with clear cells
* May secrete bile

Positive stains

* [AFP](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsafp.html) in serum
* Hepatic paraffin antigen AFP (50%, positive in classic gastric adenocarcinomas also)
* Polyclonal [CEA](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainscea.html) (canalicular pattern)
