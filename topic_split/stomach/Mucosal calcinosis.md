Definition / general

* Associated with organ transplant patients or ulcer patients taking aluminum containing antacids or sucralfate ([Am J Surg Pathol 1993;17:45](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/8447508))

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Calcium deposits resemble CMV inclusions, 40 - 250 microns, just beneath surface epithelium at foveolar tips
