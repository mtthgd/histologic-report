* Tumor and tunica
* Tumor and hilum (site of extratesticular extension in > 90%) ([Am J Clin Pathol 1999;111:534](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/10191775))
* Tumor and normal testis
* Normal testis
* Epididymis
