Definition / general

* Rare, resembles uterine tumor of same name

Case reports

* 16 month infant ([Am J Surg Pathol 1997;21:282](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/9060597))
* 24 year old man with placental site trophoblastic tumor and teratoma ([Hum Pathol 2010;41:1046](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/20381116))

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Proliferation of intermediate trophoblasts, identical to uterine tumor

* [hPL](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainshpl.html)
