Definition / general

* Nonspecific finding in benign and malignant thyroid tumors; also seen in Hashimoto thyroiditis and dyshormonogenetic goiter
* **Follicular adenoma and carcinoma**: due to empty vacuoles associated with dilated endoplasmic reticulum and Golgi ([Ultrastruct Pathol 2001;25:361](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/11758717), [Am J Surg Pathol 1988;12:240](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/3278637)) or intracellular lipid and mucin ([Acta Cytol 2002;46:757](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/12146048))
* **Follicular carcinoma with McCune-Albright syndrome**: due to uncontrolled fatty acid synthesis ([Mod Pathol 1999;12:969](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/10530562))
* **Lipid-rich cell adenoma**: due to neutral fat
* **Medullary carcinoma**: may be thyroglobulin negative ([Hum Pathol 1985;16:844](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/4018781))
* **Papillary carcinoma**: due to glycogen
* **Signet ring or lipoblast type tumors**: vacuoles may contain thyroglobulin ([Am J Surg Pathol 1985;9:705](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/4061729))
* **Undifferentiated carcinoma**: due to glycogen

Case reports

* 29 year old woman with clear cell follicular adenoma arising in ectopic thyroid tissue ([Endocr Pathol 1998;9:339](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/12114783))
* 37 year old woman with follicular carcinoma with FNA showing clear cells that were negative for thyroglobulin ([Diagn Cytopathol 2000;23:222](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/10945916))

Cytology description

* **Follicular carcinoma**:
  + Clearing due to intracellular lipid and mucin
  + Cohesive sheets of round / oval cells with abundant pale staining cytoplasm, tumor cells often cluster around branching capillaries, also frequent fat cells with discrete cell borders, cytoplasm distended by fat globules and eccentric nuclei ([Acta Cytol 2002;46:757](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/12146048))

Positive stains

* Thyroid tumors are generally thyroglobulin+ ([Am J Surg Pathol 1984;8:187](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/6703194)), but may be weak / negative
