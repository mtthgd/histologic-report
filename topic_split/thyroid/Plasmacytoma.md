Definition / general

* Rare tumor of thyroid gland, presenting as a mass
* Solitary or associated with multiple myeloma
* Diagnosis should be restricted to tumors without a lymphoid component

Case reports

* 19 year old woman ([N Z Med J 2006;119:U2005](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/16751828))

Differential diagnosis

* Plasmacytoid form of [medullary carcinoma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/thyroidmedullary.html)
* **Reactive / inflammatory conditions such as inflammatory pseudotumor**
* Thyroid involvement with [multiple myeloma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/lymphomamyeloma.html)
* Thyroid [marginal zone lymphoma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/lymphomaMALT.html) with extensive plasmacytic differentiation
* [Undifferentiated carcinoma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/thyroidUndiff.html)
