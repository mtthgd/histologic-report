Definition / general

* Occurs after FNA or surgery
* Rare, reported in other sites as genitourinary system and breast ([Am J Clin Pathol 1999;111:70](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/9894456))

Clinical features

* Thyroid mass
* History of surgery or FNA

Radiology description

* Hypoechoic mass with calcification ([Korean J Pathol 2013;47:89](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/23479514))

Case reports

* 31 year old woman with postoperative spindle cell nodule after thyroidectomy ([Head Neck 2013;35:E13](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/22106010))
* 53 year old woman with postoperative spindle cell nodule in neck after thyroidectomy ([Korean J Pathol 2013;47:89](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/23479514))

Microscopic (histologic) description

* Spindle cell proliferation
* Bland cytology, no pleomorphism
* Low mitotic count, no atypical mitoses
* Other features may be seen as scattered inflammatory cells and histiocytes, focal myxoid change and delicate blood vessels

Differential diagnosis

* Recurrence of the lesion
* Spindle cell lesions of the thyroid:
  + [Medullary carcinoma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/thyroidmedullary.html)
  + [Sarcoma](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/thyroidsarcoma.html)
  + **Schwannoma**
