Definition / general

* Recently described entity that occurs in the vulva and shares remarkable histologic overlap with lipoblastoma and myxoid liposarcoma
* These lesions have been shown to have [Rb](https://www.pathologyoutlines.com/topic/stainsrb.html) loss like the spindle cell lipoma family of tumors
* Does not have *PLAG1* or *FUS* rearrangements ([Int J Gynecol Pathol 2019;38:204](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/29300200), [Am J Surg Pathol 2015;39:1290](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/25929353))
* Has only a limited ability to locally recur and these lesions should be distinguished from myxoid liposarcoma
